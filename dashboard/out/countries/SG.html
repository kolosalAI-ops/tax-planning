Key Insight

Singapore offers one of the most competitive tax regimes globally: 17% headline CIT with effective rates as low as 4.25% for first S$10K, 0% WHT on dividends, no capital gains tax, territorial system with foreign income exemption, and extensive concessionary rates from 0-10% for qualifying activities. The Enterprise Innovation Scheme provides up to 400% R&D deduction.
