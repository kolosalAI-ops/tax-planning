Key Insight

Mexico's flat 30% CIT is partially offset by northern/southeast border zone incentives (20% effective ISR, 8% IVA) and a 30% R&D credit on incremental spending. The maquiladora safe harbor provides PE protection for foreign manufacturers. Key challenge: 10% dividend WHT on post-2013 earnings and 10% profit-sharing (PTU) obligation.
