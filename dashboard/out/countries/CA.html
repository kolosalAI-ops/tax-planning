Key Insight

Canada's combined federal-provincial rates (23-31%) are offset by powerful SR&ED credits: 35% refundable credit for CCPCs on first C$3M. Alberta offers the lowest combined rate (23%). Key advantages: extensive 90-treaty network, 0% interest WHT under treaty with US, and immediate expensing for CCPCs up to C$1.5M.
