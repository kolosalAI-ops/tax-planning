Key Insight

Australia's imputation system eliminates dividend double-taxation domestically (refundable franking credits). The 25% base rate entity rate benefits smaller companies. R&D incentives are generous: 43.5% refundable offset for SMEs. Key challenge: 40% Diverted Profits Tax targets profit-shifting, and thin capitalisation rules apply a 30% EBITDA test.
