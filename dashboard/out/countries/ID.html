Key Insight

Indonesia offers aggressive incentives for investment: 0.5% MSME final tax, up to 20-year tax holidays for large investments, and R&D super deductions up to 300%. However, the 20% WHT on dividends and profits (reducible by treaty), moderate FX controls, and mandatory BKPM registration add compliance complexity.
