Strategic Insight

Chile ($350K) and Portugal ($325K) offer the highest effective R&D benefits per $1M spend, yet are often overlooked in favour of better-known regimes. For pre-revenue startups, Canada (35% refundable) and Australia (43.5% refundable) provide unmatched cash flow support. Patent box jurisdictions like Belgium (3.75%) and Ireland (6.25%) deliver the best long-term value for IP-monetising companies. The optimal multinational structure combines refundable credits (R&D location), enhanced deductions (operating subsidiaries), and patent boxes (IP holding) across 2-3 jurisdictions.
