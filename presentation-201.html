Tax & Commercial
  • Apply for incentives (Tax Holiday, Pioneer, SR&ED)
  • Establish transfer pricing policy + documentation
  • Map treaty WHT rates for all intercompany flows
  • Register for VAT/GST if threshold is met
  • Set up payroll withholding and social contributions
  • Engage local tax counsel and file elections
Timing: Incentive applications (Tax Holiday, Pioneer Status) must be filed before operations begin. Retroactive claims are generally not available.

Repatriation & Exit Checklist

Repatriation
  • Use JCP (not dividends) for Brazil repatriation
  • Frank all Australian dividends for 0% WHT
  • Claim FSIE exemption in Singapore (ITA §13(8))
  • Maximize FTC before year-end in no-carry jurisdictions
  • Time dividend declarations to match FTC capacity
  • Consider interest/royalty channels for tax-efficient extraction
Exit & Disposal
  • Sell shares (not assets) for CGT benefits
  • Use Singapore 0% CGT for share disposals
  • Check real-property-rich rules (FIRPTA, TAP, TCP)
  • Pre-exit dividend to reduce embedded gains
  • Confirm §382/ownership change rules before M&A
  • Obtain tax clearance certificates before closing
Exit planning: Structure the exit at least 12 months in advance. Pre-exit dividends can reduce embedded gains. Route disposals through Singapore for 0% CGT wherever commercially feasible.
