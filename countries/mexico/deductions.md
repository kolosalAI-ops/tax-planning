| Parameter | Value |

## 3. Net Operating Losses (Pérdida Fiscal) - LISR Artículo 57

> **Legal basis**: LISR Artículo 57; LISR Artículo 57 párrafo 3 (inflation adjustment via INPC)

| Parameter | Value |
|---|---|
| Carry-forward period | **10 years** — LISR Art. 57 párrafo 1 |
| Carry-back | **Not allowed** |
| Limitation | **100%** of taxable income (no percentage cap, unlike some countries) |
| Inflation adjustment | Losses updated by INPC factors each year — LISR Art. 57 párrafo 3 |
| Merger/spin-off | Losses are NOT transferable to surviving/new entity — LISR Art. 57 |
| Change of shareholders | Losses may be challenged if >50% ownership change |

## 4. Interest Deduction (Deducción de Intereses)

> **Legal basis**: LISR Artículo 28 fracción XXVII (interest limitation — 30% adjusted taxable income); LISR Artículo 28 fracción XXXII (thin capitalization 3:1 DER)

### General Rules

| Parameter | Value |
|---|---|
| Business interest | Fully deductible if arm's length and for business purposes — LISR Art. 25 fracción VII |
| Net interest limitation | **30%** of adjusted taxable profit (EBITDA-based, BEPS-aligned) — LISR Art. 28 fracción XXVII |
| Excess carry-forward | **10 years** — LISR Art. 28 fracción XXVII |
| Exemption | Entities with net interest expense ≤ **MXN 20 million** |
| Financial institutions | Exempt from limitation |

### Thin Capitalization (LISR Art. 28 fracción XXXII)

| Parameter | Value |
|---|---|
| Debt-to-equity ratio | **3:1** (related-party debt) — LISR Art. 28 fracción XXXII |
| Excess interest | **Non-deductible** |
| Back-to-back loans | Subject to related-party rules |
| Computation | Based on average daily balances |

### Related-Party Interest

| Parameter | Value |
|---|---|
| Arm's length requirement | Interest rate must be at market rate — CFF Art. 179 |
| Documentation | Transfer pricing study required — CFF Arts. 76-A, 76-B |
| WHT rate | 4.9% - 35% depending on beneficiary — LISR Art. 166 |

## 9. Bad Debt Deduction (Créditos Incobrables) - LISR Art. 27 fracción VI; Art. 28 fracción XIII

> **Legal basis**: LISR Artículo 27 fracción VI (bad debt deduction requirements); LISR Artículo 28 fracción XIII (bad debt limitations)

| Amount | Requirements for Deduction |
|---|---|
| Up to **~MXN 30,000 UDIS** (~MXN 240,000) | Debt is ≥12 months overdue AND debtor notified of write-off AND reported to credit bureau |
| Above MXN 30,000 UDIS | All of the above PLUS judicial collection process initiated |
| Related-party debts | Must obtain official insolvency determination |
| Public debtor | Government declaration of inability to pay |

### Timing

| Parameter | Value |
|---|---|
| Deduction year | Year when all requirements are met |
| Prescription | Must claim within 5 years of meeting requirements |
| Recovery | If recovered after deduction, taxable as income |

## 13. Research & Development

Mexico offers a 30% tax credit on incremental qualifying R&D expenditures under LISR Art. 202 (EFIDT program), capped at MXN 50 million per year, with a 10-year carry-forward for unused credits; the increment is measured against the average of the prior three years and certification by CONAHCYT is required. In addition, qualifying R&D expenditures are fully deductible as ordinary business expenses under LISR Art. 25 fracción XII in the year incurred.

See [R&D Incentives](rnd_incentives.md) for full program detail.

## 14. Provisions & Reserves

> **Legal basis**: LISR Artículo 28 fracción V (general non-deductibility of provisions and reserves)

| Provision Type | Treatment | Legal Reference |
|---|---|---|
| General provisions and reserves | **Not deductible** — must be added back | LISR Art. 28 fracción V |
| Warranty reserves | **Not deductible** until actual claim is paid | LISR Art. 28 fracción V |
| Contingency reserves | **Not deductible** until liability crystallises | LISR Art. 28 fracción V |
| Bad debt reserves (general) | **Not deductible** — specific write-off rules apply per Art. 27 fracción VI | LISR Art. 28 fracción XIII |
| Employee severance accruals | **Deductible** only when actually paid | LISR Art. 28 fracción V |
| Vacation accruals | **Deductible** when paid | LISR Art. 25 fracción III |

> Mexico follows a strict cash/accrual hybrid system where most provisions are non-deductible until the underlying obligation is settled.
