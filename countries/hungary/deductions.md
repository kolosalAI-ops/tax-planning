# Hungary - Taxable Income Deductions

> **Jurisdiction**: National (Nemzeti Ado- es Vamhivatal — NAV)
> **Governing Law**: 1996. evi LXXXI. torveny a tarsasagi adorol es az osztalekadorol (TAO) — Corporate Tax Act
> **Tax Year**: 2024/2025
> **Currency**: HUF (Ft)

---

## 1. Depreciation & Capital Allowances

Hungary's tax depreciation rules are set out in TAO §1-2 and Annex 1. Tax depreciation is calculated independently from accounting depreciation. The taxpayer must adjust accounting profit for differences between accounting and tax depreciation.

### 1.1 Standard Depreciation Rates — TAO Annex 1

| Asset Type | Annual Rate | Statutory Basis |
|---|---|---|
| **Buildings (general)** | 2% | TAO Annex 1, Point 1 |
| **Buildings (prefabricated/light structure)** | 3% | TAO Annex 1, Point 1 |
| **Buildings (temporary structures)** | 15% | TAO Annex 1, Point 1 |
| **Machinery & equipment (general)** | 14.5% | TAO Annex 1, Point 2 |
| **Production machinery** | 14.5% | TAO Annex 1, Point 2 |
| **Vehicles (passenger cars)** | 20% | TAO Annex 1, Point 3 |
| **Vehicles (trucks)** | 20% | TAO Annex 1, Point 3 |
| **Computers & IT equipment** | 33% | TAO Annex 1, Point 4 |
| **Office furniture & fixtures** | 14.5% | TAO Annex 1, Point 2 |
| **Intellectual property (acquired patents, licenses)** | Per useful life (min 2 years) | TAO Annex 1, Point 5 |
| **Goodwill** | Per useful life or 10% | TAO Annex 1, Point 5 |
| **Software** | 33% | TAO Annex 1, Point 4 |
| **Telecommunications equipment** | 14.5% | TAO Annex 1, Point 2 |

### 1.2 Depreciation Method

| Parameter | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Method** | Straight-line only — TAO Annex 1 |
| **Start** | From the date the asset is placed in service |
| **Residual value** | Depreciation continues until fully written off |
| **Land** | Not depreciable |

### 1.3 Low-Value Assets — TAO §1 Annex 1

| Parameter | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Threshold** | Assets with acquisition cost ≤HUF 200,000 may be fully expensed in the year of acquisition [unverified — confirm with local counsel] |
| **Small-value tangible assets** | Follow accounting treatment; 100% deduction if classified as small-value under accounting rules |

### 1.4 Accelerated Depreciation

| Scenario | Detail | Statutory Basis |
|---|---|---|
| **Government-designated priority investments** | 50% accelerated rate available for qualifying investments in priority areas [unverified — confirm with local counsel] | TAO §1 Annex 1 special provisions |
| **Film/media production assets** | Special rates apply for qualifying media production equipment | TAO Annex 1 |

## 2. Business Operating Expenses

General deductibility follows the principle that expenses must be incurred in connection with business activities. Hungary's corporate tax is calculated by adjusting accounting profit (pre-tax result) with specific increasing and decreasing items set out in TAO §7-8.

### 2.1 Fully Deductible Expenses

| Expense Category | Deductibility | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| **Employee salaries & wages** | 100% | Including bonuses |
| **Employer social contributions** | 100% | 13% social contribution tax (szocialis hozzajarulasi ado) — SzHO tv. |
| **Rent (business premises)** | 100% | Must be business use |
| **Insurance premiums** | 100% | Business-related risks |
| **Professional fees** | 100% | Legal, audit, consulting |
| **Advertising & marketing** | 100% | Business purpose required |
| **Travel expenses** | 100% | Business travel; per diem rates apply |
| **Training expenses** | 100% | Job-related employee training |
| **Bad debts (realized)** | 100% | Subject to conditions — TAO §7 (1) n |

### 2.2 Partially Deductible / Restricted

| Expense Category | Limit | Statutory Basis |
|---|---|---|
| **Entertainment & representation** | Not deductible for CIT purposes (adds back to tax base) | TAO §8 (1) d |
| **Company cars (private use element)** | Benefit-in-kind taxed; employer cost deductible | Szja tv. (PIT Act) §70 |
| **Charitable donations** | Deductible within limits; qualifying donations reduce tax base | TAO §7 (1) z |
| **Membership fees** | Deductible if business-related | General principle |

## 3. Interest & Financing Costs

### 3.1 General Deductibility

Interest expenses are generally deductible if incurred in connection with business activities.

### 3.2 EBITDA-Based Interest Limitation — TAO §8 (1) dz

| Parameter | Detail |
|---|---|
| **General rule** | Excess borrowing costs deductible up to 30% of tax EBITDA — TAO §8 (1) dz |
| **De minimis** | HUF 939,810,000 (approximately €2.5M at historic exchange rate [unverified — confirm with local counsel]) — excess borrowing costs up to this threshold are always deductible — TAO §8 (1) dz |
| **Definition** | Excess borrowing costs = deductible borrowing costs minus taxable borrowing income |
| **Carry-forward** | Non-deductible interest may be carried forward indefinitely — TAO §8 (1) dz |
| **Scope** | Applies to all borrowing costs (related and unrelated party) |
| **Stand-alone entities** | Stand-alone entities exempt from the limitation |

### 3.3 Thin Capitalization — TAO §8 (1) j

| Parameter | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Debt-to-equity ratio** | 3:1 — interest on liabilities exceeding 3× adjusted equity is non-deductible — TAO §8 (1) j |
| **Scope** | Applies to all liabilities including bank loans (not limited to related-party) — TAO §8 (1) j |
| **Interaction** | Both thin capitalization (3:1) and EBITDA rules apply independently; interest denied under either rule is non-deductible |

### 3.4 Related-Party Debt — TAO §18

| Parameter | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Arm's length** | Related-party transactions must be at arm's length — TAO §18 |
| **Related party definition** | Majority ownership or common control — TAO §4 (23) |

## 4. Research & Development

Hungary provides a development tax allowance (fejlesztesi adokedvezmeny) under TAO §22/B for qualifying R&D and investment projects. Companies may also claim an R&D cost deduction under TAO §7 (1) t, which allows the deduction of direct R&D costs from the corporate tax base. The R&D deduction may be doubled (200% deduction) for costs related to basic research and applied research conducted with higher education or research institutions.

See [R&D Incentives](rnd_incentives.md) for full program detail.

## 5. Loss Carry-Forward & Carry-Back

### 5.1 Loss Carry-Forward — TAO §17

| Parameter | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Duration** | **Indefinite** carry-forward (the former time limitation was abolished; losses declared in previous years may be offset without time restriction from the 2025 tax year onward) — TAO §17 as amended |
| **Annual cap** | **50%** of current year's taxable income (before loss offset) — TAO §17 (1a) |
| **Example** | If pre-loss taxable income = HUF 100M: maximum loss offset = HUF 50M |
| **Ownership change** | Tax authority approval required if >50% ownership change occurs — TAO §17 (9) |
| **Merger** | Losses may transfer in qualifying mergers subject to NAV approval — TAO §17 (8) |

### 5.2 Loss Carry-Back

| Parameter | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Carry-back** | **Not permitted** under Hungarian law |

### 5.3 Group Taxation

| Parameter | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Group taxation** | Optional group CIT regime available since 2019 — TAO §2/A |
| **Effect** | Group members can offset profits and losses within the group for the current year |
| **Requirement** | ≥75% ownership; Hungarian-resident entities — TAO §2/A (1) |
| **Administration** | Designated group representative files on behalf of group |

## 6. Provisions & Reserves

### 6.1 Development Reserve (Fejlesztesi Tartalek) — TAO §7 (1) f

| Parameter | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Maximum allocation** | 50% of pre-tax profit, up to HUF 10Bn per year — TAO §7 (1) f |
| **Purpose** | Reserve for future qualifying investments in tangible assets |
| **Usage period** | Must be used for qualifying investments within 4 years of allocation — TAO §7 (1) f |
| **Effect** | Reduces corporate tax base in the year of allocation |
| **Non-use** | If reserve is not used within 4 years, it must be reversed into the tax base plus a 20% penalty surcharge — TAO §7 (15) [unverified — confirm rate with local counsel] |

### 6.2 Bad Debt Provisions — TAO §7 (1) n

| Parameter | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Tax base deduction** | 50% of the book value of doubtful receivables may reduce the tax base — TAO §7 (1) n |
| **Conditions** | Receivable must be overdue >365 days, debtor must be unrelated party |
| **Full write-off** | 100% deductible upon definitive establishment of uncollectibility |
| **Related-party debts** | Provisions for related-party receivables are not deductible |

### 6.3 Inventory Valuation

| Parameter | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Method** | Per accounting standards (Hungarian Accounting Act — Szamviteli tv.) |
| **Write-down** | Accounting write-downs for impairment are generally followed for tax |
| **FIFO / weighted average** | Both accepted |

### 6.4 Other Provisions

| Parameter | Detail |
|---|---|
| **General provisions** | Accounting provisions generally not deductible for tax; tax base is adjusted for differences between accounting and tax treatment |
| **Warranty** | Not deductible as a provision; deductible when realized |
| **Environmental** | Specific deductions may apply under environmental regulations |

## 7. Industry-Specific Deductions

### 7.1 Development Tax Allowance (Fejlesztesi Adokedvezmeny) — TAO §22/B

| Parameter | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Benefit** | Tax credit of up to 80% of CIT liability (small/micro enterprises: 80%; medium: 70%; large: 50%) |
| **Qualifying investments** | New manufacturing, R&D, environmental, energy efficiency, film production investments |
| **Minimum investment** | Thresholds vary by investment type and region (e.g., HUF 100M for general investments [unverified — confirm with local counsel]) |
| **Duration** | Credit usable over up to 13 years following the investment |
| **Pre-approval** | Must be pre-approved by the Ministry of Finance |

### 7.2 Film & Media Production

| Deduction | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Indirect support** | Companies may provide subsidies to film producers and deduct the contribution from their tax base (up to their CIT liability) |
| **Rate** | 25% of qualifying Hungarian production costs [unverified — confirm with local counsel] — TAO §24/A |

### 7.3 Energy-Intensive Industries

| Deduction | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Mining royalties** | Deductible as business expenses |
| **Environmental compliance** | Qualifying environmental expenditures may be eligible for accelerated depreciation |

### 7.4 Sports Sponsorship — TAO §22/C

| Parameter | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Benefit** | Companies may allocate up to 80% of CIT liability to qualifying sports organizations |
| **Additional benefit** | The contributed amount also reduces the CIT tax base (complementary tax base reduction) |
| **Qualifying sports** | Football, handball, basketball, water polo, ice hockey |

### 7.5 Free Zones / Special Economic Zones

| Parameter | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Status** | Hungary does not have formal free trade zones for CIT purposes; incentives are provided through the development tax allowance system |

## 8. Treaties & Transfer Pricing

### 8.1 Transfer Pricing — TAO §18

| Parameter | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Arm's length principle** | Mandatory for all related-party transactions — TAO §18 (1) |
| **Documentation** | Contemporaneous TP documentation required for transactions exceeding HUF 50M — TAO §18 (5) |
| **Country-by-Country Reporting** | Required for groups with consolidated revenue ≥ HUF 265Bn (approximately €750M) — TAO §18 (5d) |
| **Master File / Local File** | Required following OECD BEPS Action 13 guidelines |
| **Penalties** | Default penalty of HUF 2M per missing TP documentation; HUF 4M for repeated non-compliance — Art. §172 (2) of the Tax Procedure Act |

### 8.2 Accepted Methods

1. Comparable Uncontrolled Price (CUP)
2. Resale Price Method
3. Cost Plus Method
4. Transactional Net Margin Method (TNMM)
5. Profit Split Method

### 8.3 Tax Treaties

| Parameter | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Treaty network** | Hungary has 80+ double taxation treaties |
| **Model** | Based on OECD Model Tax Convention |
| **MAP** | Mutual Agreement Procedure available |
| **EU Arbitration Convention** | Applicable for intra-EU disputes |
| **MLI** | Hungary has ratified the Multilateral Instrument (MLI) |

### 8.4 Withholding Tax (Outbound)

| Payment Type | Domestic Rate | Treaty-Reduced Rate |
|---|---|---|
| **Dividends** | 0% (no WHT on dividends to corporate recipients) | 0% |
| **Interest** | 0% (no WHT on interest under domestic law) | 0% |
| **Royalties** | 0% (no WHT on royalties under domestic law) | 0% |

**Note:** Hungary does not levy withholding tax on dividends, interest, or royalties paid to corporate recipients. WHT of 15% applies only to dividends paid to non-resident individuals.

## 9. Non-Deductible Items

### 9.1 Expressly Non-Deductible / Tax Base Increasing Items — TAO §8

| Expense | Reason | Statutory Basis |
|---|---|---|
| **Fines and penalties** | Not incurred for business purposes | TAO §8 (1) a |
| **Bribes and corrupt payments** | Prohibited by law | TAO §8 (1) a |
| **Income taxes (CIT)** | Not a deductible expense | General principle |
| **Entertainment & representation** | Added back to tax base | TAO §8 (1) d |
| **Non-business expenses** | Expenses not connected to entrepreneurial activity | TAO §8 (1) d |
| **Excess depreciation** | Accounting depreciation exceeding tax depreciation rates | TAO §8 (1) b |
| **Dividends paid** | Distribution of profits | N/A |
| **Unused development reserve** | Reversed plus 20% penalty | TAO §7 (15) |
| **Related-party bad debt provisions** | Not deductible | TAO §7 (1) n |
| **Thin capitalization excess interest** | Interest on liabilities exceeding 3× equity | TAO §8 (1) j |
| **Excess borrowing costs above EBITDA** | Above HUF 939.81M / 30% EBITDA | TAO §8 (1) dz |
| **Non-arm's length pricing adjustments** | Transfer pricing adjustments increase tax base | TAO §18 |
| **Impairment losses (certain)** | Accounting impairment not always followed for tax | TAO §8 (1) gy |

### 9.2 Participation Exemption

| Parameter | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Dividends received** | Generally exempt from CIT (not included in tax base) — TAO §7 (1) g |
| **Capital gains on reported participations** | Tax-exempt if the holding was reported to NAV as a "reported participation" (bejelentett reszesedes) at acquisition — TAO §7 (1) dz |
| **Conditions** | ≥10% holding; held for ≥1 year; reported within 75 days of acquisition — TAO §4 (5) |
| **Effect on losses** | Capital losses on reported participations are non-deductible — TAO §8 (1) m |
