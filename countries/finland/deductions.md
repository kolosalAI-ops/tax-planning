# Finland - Taxable Income Deductions

> **Jurisdiction**: National (Vero / Finnish Tax Administration)
> **Governing Law**: Laki elinkeinotulon verottamisesta (EVL) — Business Income Tax Act
> **Tax Year**: 2024/2025
> **Currency**: EUR (€)

---

## 1. Depreciation & Capital Allowances

Finland applies declining balance depreciation for most tangible assets used in business operations. The rules are codified in EVL §§30-45.

### 1.1 Machinery & Equipment — EVL §30

| Parameter | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Method** | Declining balance — EVL §30 |
| **Maximum rate** | 25% per year — EVL §30 |
| **Accelerated rate (2020-2025)** | 50% declining balance for newly acquired machinery/equipment — Laki koneiden, kaluston ja muun niihin verrattavan irtaimen käyttöomaisuuden korotetuista poistoista verovuosina 2020-2025 |
| **Pooling** | All machinery and equipment are pooled — EVL §30 |
| **Disposal** | Proceeds reduce the pool balance |
| **Low-value assets** | Assets with acquisition cost ≤€1,200 may be fully expensed in the year of acquisition — EVL §33 |
| **Small items** | Items with useful life ≤3 years may be fully expensed — EVL §33 |

### 1.2 Buildings — EVL §34

| Building Type | Annual Rate | Statutory Basis |
|---|---|---|
| **Industrial buildings (factories, workshops)** | 7% declining balance | EVL §34 (2) 1 |
| **Office buildings** | 4% declining balance | EVL §34 (2) 2 |
| **Retail/commercial buildings** | 4% declining balance | EVL §34 (2) 2 |
| **Residential buildings (rental)** | 4% declining balance | EVL §34 (2) 2 |
| **Warehouses** | 7% declining balance | EVL §34 (2) 1 |
| **Lightweight structures (temporary)** | 20% declining balance | EVL §34 (2) 3 |

### 1.3 Intangible Assets

| Asset Type | Rate / Method | Statutory Basis |
|---|---|---|
| **Goodwill (acquired)** | Straight-line over 10 years (max) | EVL §37 |
| **Patents (acquired)** | Straight-line over useful life (or remaining patent life) | EVL §37 |
| **Licenses** | Straight-line over useful life | EVL §37 |
| **Self-developed intangibles** | Expensed as incurred | EVL §22 |
| **Long-term expenditure (multi-year)** | Amortized over impact period, max 10 years | EVL §24 |

### 1.4 Land Improvements

| Parameter | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Rate** | Not depreciable (land is a non-depreciable asset) |
| **Land improvements** | Depreciable under specific provisions if qualifying — EVL §35 |

## 2. Business Operating Expenses

General deductibility is governed by EVL §7 — expenses incurred for the purpose of acquiring or maintaining business income are deductible.

### 2.1 Fully Deductible Expenses — EVL §7

| Expense Category | Deductibility | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| **Employee salaries & wages** | 100% | Including bonuses, holiday pay |
| **Employer social security contributions** | 100% | Including pension (TyEL), unemployment, health insurance contributions |
| **Rent (business premises)** | 100% | Must be for business use |
| **Insurance premiums** | 100% | Business-related risks |
| **Professional fees** | 100% | Legal, audit, consulting |
| **Advertising & marketing** | 100% | Business purpose required |
| **Travel expenses** | 100% | Business travel; per diem rates set by Vero annually |
| **Training expenses** | 100% | Job-related employee training |
| **Bad debts (realized)** | 100% | Must be definitively established as uncollectible — EVL §17 |

### 2.2 Partially Deductible / Restricted

| Expense Category | Limit | Statutory Basis |
|---|---|---|
| **Entertainment (customer)** | 50% deductible | EVL §8 (1) 8 |
| **Gifts to business contacts** | Deductible if reasonable and customary | EVL §7 (general principle) |
| **Pension contributions** | Deductible within statutory TyEL contribution rates | TyEL (Employee Pension Act) |
| **Charitable donations** | Deductible €850-€250,000 per year to approved recipients — EVL §57 | EVL §57 |

## 3. Interest & Financing Costs

### 3.1 General Deductibility — EVL §18

Interest expenses are generally deductible on an accrual basis if incurred for business purposes — EVL §18 (1) 2.

### 3.2 EBITDA-Based Interest Limitation — EVL §18a

| Parameter | Detail |
|---|---|
| **General rule** | Net interest expenses deductible up to 25% of tax EBITDA — EVL §18a (2) |
| **De minimis** | €500,000 — net interest expenses up to €500,000 are always deductible — EVL §18a (3) |
| **Scope** | Applies to net interest to related parties; third-party interest generally not limited — EVL §18a (1) |
| **Carry-forward** | Non-deductible interest cannot be carried forward (lost permanently) |
| **Equity escape** | If the entity's equity ratio equals or exceeds the consolidated group's equity ratio, the limitation does not apply — EVL §18a (4) |
| **Stand-alone entities** | Exemption for entities not part of a group — EVL §18a (1) |
| **Public infrastructure** | Exemption for long-term public infrastructure project companies — EVL §18a (5) |

### 3.3 Anti-Hybrid Rules — EVL §18c-18e

| Parameter | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Implementation** | ATAD hybrid mismatch rules — EVL §§18c-18e |
| **Effect** | Deductions denied for payments creating deduction/no-inclusion outcomes |
| **Scope** | Related-party and structured arrangements |

## 4. Research & Development

Finland provides an additional R&D tax deduction under a regime introduced in 2023 (effective from tax year 2023). Qualifying companies can claim an extra deduction of 50% on qualifying R&D expenses (wages and purchased R&D services), in addition to the normal 100% deduction, resulting in a total 150% deduction. A further combined deduction of up to 200% may apply for collaboration with research institutions [unverified — confirm with local counsel].

See [R&D Incentives](rnd_incentives.md) for full program detail.

## 5. Loss Carry-Forward & Carry-Back

### 5.1 Loss Carry-Forward — EVL §119

| Parameter | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Duration** | **10 years** — EVL §119 (1) |
| **Annual cap** | **No cap** — 100% of taxable income may be offset |
| **Ownership change restriction** | Losses forfeited if >50% of shares change ownership (directly or indirectly) during the loss year or after — EVL §122 |
| **Dispensation** | The Tax Administration may grant permission to use losses despite ownership change if continuation of business is justified — EVL §122 (3) |
| **Income source separation** | The separate income source system (EVL business income vs. TVL other income) was significantly reformed by Laki elinkeinotulon verottamisesta 30.12.2019/1401 effective 2020. For most corporate taxpayers, the separate income source distinction for passive income was abolished so that losses from one source may now generally offset income from the other — EVL §2a [unverified — confirm current application with local counsel] |

### 5.2 Loss Carry-Back

| Parameter | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Carry-back** | **Not permitted** under Finnish law |

### 5.3 Group Contributions (Konsernavdrag) — Konsernavdragslag (825/1986)

| Parameter | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Availability** | Between Finnish parent and ≥90%-owned Finnish subsidiaries — Konsernavdragslag §3 |
| **Effect** | Deductible for payer, taxable for recipient |
| **Requirement** | Group relationship must have existed for the entire fiscal year |
| **Cross-border** | Not available to foreign group entities (domestic only) |
| **Minimum amount** | Contribution must be at least €20,000 [unverified — confirm with local counsel] |

## 6. Provisions & Reserves

### 6.1 Bad Debt Provisions — EVL §17

| Parameter | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Realized losses** | Fully deductible when loss is definitively established — EVL §17 (2) |
| **Provisions** | Deductible if the receivable is individually assessed and loss is considered probable — EVL §17 (2) |
| **General provisions** | Not deductible — must be specific |
| **Bankruptcy of debtor** | Creates presumption of loss |

### 6.2 Inventory Valuation — EVL §28

| Parameter | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Method** | Lower of acquisition cost, replacement cost, or probable selling price — EVL §28 |
| **FIFO** | Generally applied |
| **Obsolescence** | Write-down permitted for demonstrably obsolete items |
| **Work-in-progress** | Valued at direct costs minimum |

### 6.3 Warranty Provisions

| Parameter | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Deductibility** | Warranty provisions are generally deductible when based on specific warranty obligations and historical experience — EVL §48 |
| **Requirement** | Must be connected to specific products or services and estimable with reasonable accuracy |

### 6.4 Restructuring Provisions

| Parameter | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Deductibility** | Restructuring provisions are generally not deductible until costs are realized |

## 8. Treaties & Transfer Pricing

### 8.1 Transfer Pricing — Verotusmenettelylaki (VML) §31

| Parameter | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Arm's length principle** | Mandatory for all related-party transactions — VML §31 |
| **Documentation** | Contemporaneous TP documentation required — VML §14a-14c |
| **Country-by-Country Reporting** | Required for groups with consolidated revenue ≥ €750M — VML §14d |
| **Master File / Local File** | Required following OECD BEPS Action 13 guidelines |
| **Penalties** | Tax increase (veronkorotus) up to 30% on TP adjustments — VML §32 |

## 9. Non-Deductible Items

### 9.1 Expressly Non-Deductible — EVL §16

| Expense | Reason | Statutory Basis |
|---|---|---|
| **Fines and penalties** | Not incurred for business purposes | EVL §16 (5) |
| **Bribes and corrupt payments** | Prohibited by law | EVL §16 (8) |
| **Income taxes (Finnish and foreign)** | Not a deductible expense | EVL §16 (3) |
| **Dividends paid** | Distribution of profits | N/A |
| **50% of entertainment expenses** | Statutory cap at 50% deduction | EVL §8 (1) 8 |
| **Private/personal expenses** | Not business-related | EVL §16 (1) |
| **Donations (outside statutory limits)** | Only €850-€250,000 to approved charities deductible | EVL §57 |
| **Capital losses on qualifying shares** | Participation exemption applies | EVL §6b |
| **Tax-exempt income related expenses** | Expenses attributable to tax-exempt income are non-deductible | EVL §16 (7) |
| **Excessive interest** | Interest exceeding EBITDA limitation | EVL §18a |

### 9.2 Participation Exemption — EVL §6a-6b

| Parameter | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Qualifying holdings** | Shares representing ≥10% held for ≥1 year; both entities are EU/EEA corporations or treaty-country entities — EVL §6a |
| **Effect** | Capital gains and dividends on qualifying shares are tax-exempt; losses are non-deductible — EVL §6b |
| **Listed shares** | Participation exemption applies only to unlisted subsidiary shares (>10%); listed subsidiary dividends are 75% taxable if <10% holding |
