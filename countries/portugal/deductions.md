# Portugal - Taxable Income Deductions

> **Jurisdiction**: National (AT — Autoridade Tributária e Aduaneira)
> **Governing Law**: Código do Imposto sobre o Rendimento das Pessoas Coletivas (CIRC)
> **Tax Year**: 2024/2025
> **Currency**: EUR (€)

---

## 1. Depreciation & Capital Allowances

### General Principles — Art. 29–34 CIRC

Tax depreciation (amortizações e depreciações) is computed using rates prescribed in Decreto Regulamentar 25/2009 (DR 25/2009). Depreciation is mandatory — failure to claim results in permanent loss of the allowance for that year.

### Depreciation Methods — Art. 30 CIRC

| Method | Availability | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Straight-line (quotas constantes) | Default method | Art. 30.1 CIRC |
| Declining balance (quotas degressivas) | Available for new tangible assets (not buildings, passenger vehicles, or office furniture) with useful life ≥3 years | Art. 30.2 CIRC |

### Declining Balance Coefficients — Art. 30.2 CIRC

| Useful Life | Coefficient (× straight-line rate) |
|---|---|
| <5 years | **1.5** |
| 5–6 years | **2.0** |
| ≥7 years | **2.5** |

### Key Depreciation Rates — DR 25/2009 Tables

| Asset Category | Maximum Rate | Minimum Useful Life | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| Industrial buildings | **5%** | 20 years | |
| Commercial buildings | **2%** | 50 years | |
| Light industrial buildings | **5–10%** | 10–20 years | |
| Machinery and equipment | **12.5–20%** | 5–8 years | Industry-specific |
| IT equipment (hardware) | **20–33.3%** | 3–5 years | |
| Software | **33.3%** | 3 years | |
| Motor vehicles (light passenger) | **25%** | 4 years | Subject to cost cap — Art. 34 CIRC |
| Office furniture | **12.5%** | 8 years | |
| Intangible assets (finite life) | Over contractual or useful life | Varies | Art. 16 DR 25/2009 |
| Goodwill | Not depreciable for tax unless from arm's-length acquisition | — | Art. 16 DR 25/2009 |

### Vehicle Depreciation Caps — Art. 34 CIRC

| Vehicle Type | Maximum Depreciable Cost |
|---|---|
| Light passenger vehicles (general) | **€25,000** |
| Vehicles powered by LPG/CNG | **€37,500** |
| Plug-in hybrid vehicles | **€50,000** |
| Electric vehicles | **€62,500** |

### Accelerated Depreciation

| Parameter | Value |
|---|---|
| Shift work (extra shifts) | Additional 25% (2 shifts) or 50% (3 shifts) on standard rates — Art. 31 CIRC |
| Extraordinary wear | Increased rates allowed with tax authority approval |

## 2. Business Operating Expenses

### General Deductibility — Art. 23 CIRC

Expenses are deductible if they are incurred to obtain or guarantee taxable income, are adequately documented, and are effectively connected to the business activity.

### Key Deductible Operating Expenses

| Expense Category | Deductibility | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Employee salaries and wages | Fully deductible | Must be arm's-length for related parties |
| Employer social security contributions (TSU) | Fully deductible | Standard rate 23.75% |
| Rent and lease payments | Fully deductible | |
| Professional fees | Fully deductible | |
| Repairs and maintenance | Deductible if revenue nature | |
| Bad debts | Deductible when specific impairment recognised or debt written off | Art. 28-A–28-C CIRC |
| Insurance premiums | Fully deductible | |
| Training expenses | Fully deductible | |
| Travel and subsistence | Deductible within approved daily allowance limits | |

### Charitable Donations — Art. 62 Estatuto dos Benefícios Fiscais (EBF)

| Donation Type | Tax Benefit |
|---|---|
| Social and cultural patronage | Cost × **140%** deductible (bonus deduction); max 0.8% of revenue |
| Scientific and educational patronage | Cost × **150%** deductible |
| Environmental patronage | Cost × **120%** deductible |
| Overall cap | **0.8%** of turnover (8/1000) |

## 3. Interest & Financing Costs

### ATAD Interest Limitation — Art. 67 CIRC

| Parameter | Value |
|---|---|
| Deductible limit | Net interest expense up to the greater of: (i) **30%** of tax-EBITDA, or (ii) **€1,000,000** |
| De minimis | **€1,000,000** net interest expense |
| Carry-forward of excess interest | **5 years** |
| EBITDA carry-forward | Unused EBITDA carried forward **5 years** |
| Group | Computed per entity; RETGS groups may apply consolidated |

### Thin Capitalisation — Art. 67 CIRC (Pre-ATAD Legacy)

| Parameter | Value |
|---|---|
| Status | The ATAD-aligned rules in Art. 67 CIRC have largely superseded the former thin-cap rules |
| Non-resident related-party debt | Interest non-deductible where debt/equity ratio exceeds **2:1** (for entities in jurisdictions without a tax treaty) — Art. 67.1 CIRC [unverified — confirm with local counsel] |

## 4. Research & Development

Portugal provides the SIFIDE II (Sistema de Incentivos Fiscais à Investigação e Desenvolvimento Empresarial) tax credit, a mixed base/incremental system offering volume and incremental rates on qualifying R&D expenditure. Additionally, the RFAI (Regime Fiscal de Apoio ao Investimento) provides tax credits for productive investments in eligible regions.

See [R&D Incentives](rnd_incentives.md) for full program detail.

## 5. Loss Carry-Forward & Carry-Back

### Loss Carry-Forward — Art. 52 CIRC

| Parameter | Value |
|---|---|
| Duration | **12 years** (increased from 5 years to 12 years; applicable to losses from 2023 onward) [unverified — confirm with local counsel] |
| Restriction | Losses offset up to **65%** of taxable income per year (reduced from 70% — effective from 2023) [unverified — confirm with local counsel] |
| Change of ownership | Losses forfeited if ≥50% ownership change, unless tax authority authorisation obtained — Art. 52.8 CIRC |

### Loss Carry-Back

| Parameter | Value |
|---|---|
| Status | **Not available** under Portuguese tax law |

### RETGS (Tax Group) Losses — Art. 71 CIRC

| Parameter | Value |
|---|---|
| Pre-group losses | Can only offset against own profits within the group |
| Group losses | Offset against group aggregate profits |

## 6. Provisions & Reserves

### Tax-Deductible Provisions — Art. 39 CIRC

| Provision Type | Deductibility | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Bad debt provisions | Deductible based on ageing of receivables (progressive rates) | Art. 28-B CIRC |
| Warranty provisions | Deductible | If based on historical cost data |
| Litigation provisions | Deductible | If court proceedings initiated |
| Environmental provisions | Deductible | If legal obligation exists — Art. 39.1.c CIRC |
| Employee benefits (holiday pay, bonus) | Deductible | When accrued |
| General provisions | **Not deductible** | |

### Bad Debt Provision Ageing Schedule — Art. 28-B CIRC

| Period Overdue | Provision Rate |
|---|---|
| 6–12 months | **25%** |
| 12–18 months | **50%** |
| 18–24 months | **75%** |
| >24 months | **100%** |

### Tax-Free Reserves

| Reserve | Detail |
|---|---|
| Reinvestment reserve | Capital gains on tangible fixed assets reinvested within prescribed period (2 years prior / 3 years after) may be deferred — Art. 48 CIRC |

## 7. Industry-Specific Deductions

### RFAI (Regime Fiscal de Apoio ao Investimento) — Art. 22–26 CFI (Código Fiscal do Investimento)

| Parameter | Value |
|---|---|
| Credit rate (Lisbon region) | **10%** of eligible investment |
| Credit rate (other regions) | **25%** of eligible investment up to €15M; **10%** above |
| Eligible investments | Tangible and intangible assets for new projects or capacity expansion |
| Cap | Cannot exceed 25% of total CIT payable |

### DLRR (Deduction for Retained and Reinvested Earnings) — Art. 27–34 CFI

| Parameter | Value |

## 8. Treaties & Transfer Pricing

### Transfer Pricing — Art. 63 CIRC

| Parameter | Detail |
|---|---|
| Standard | Arm's-length principle — Art. 63.1 CIRC |
| Documentation | Transfer pricing file required annually for entities exceeding thresholds — Portaria 268/2021 |
| Thresholds | Revenue > €3M or related-party transactions > €100,000 |
| Country-by-Country Reporting | Required for MNE groups with consolidated revenue ≥ €750M — Art. 121-A CIRC |
| Penalties | Adjustments taxed normally; penalties of 5–20% on adjustments for documentation failures |
| APAs | Bilateral and unilateral available — Art. 138 CIRC |

### Participation Exemption — Art. 51-C CIRC

| Parameter | Value |
|---|---|
| Dividends | Exempt if ≥10% holding for ≥1 year and subsidiary subject to comparable tax (≥60% of standard rate) |
| Capital gains | Exempt under same conditions — Art. 51-C CIRC |

### Treaty Network

| Income Type | Typical Treaty Rate |
|---|---|

| Dividends (portfolio) | 15% |

| Royalties | 0–10% |
