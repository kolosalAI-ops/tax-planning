# Austria - Taxable Income Deductions

> **Jurisdiction**: Federal (BMF — Bundesministerium für Finanzen)
> **Governing Law**: Körperschaftsteuergesetz 1988 (KStG 1988); Einkommensteuergesetz 1988 (EStG 1988)
> **Tax Year**: 2024/2025
> **Currency**: EUR (€)

---

## 1. Depreciation & Capital Allowances

### General Principles — §7–8 EStG 1988

Tax depreciation (Absetzung für Abnutzung — AfA) is computed on a straight-line basis over the estimated useful life of the asset. Declining balance depreciation was reintroduced for certain assets.

### Depreciation Methods

| Method | Availability | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Straight-line (linear) | Default for all assets | §7 Abs. 1 EStG 1988 |
| Declining balance (degressiv) | Available for assets acquired after 1 July 2020; maximum **30%** rate (not available for buildings, goodwill, used assets, or intangibles) | §7 Abs. 1a EStG 1988 |

### Key Depreciation Rates

| Asset Category | Useful Life | Annual Rate (Straight-Line) | Statutory Basis |
|---|---|---|---|
| Commercial buildings (business use) | 40 years | **2.5%** | §8 Abs. 1 EStG 1988 |
| Industrial/factory buildings | 33 years | **3%** [unverified — confirm with local counsel] | §8 Abs. 1 EStG 1988 |
| Rental buildings (residential) | 67 years | **1.5%** | §16 Abs. 1 Z 8 EStG 1988 |
| Machinery and equipment | 5–10 years | 10–20% | Based on useful life |
| Office furniture | 10 years | 10% | |
| Computer equipment and software | 3 years | **33.3%** | BMF guidance |
| Motor vehicles | 8 years | **12.5%** | |
| Goodwill (from asset deals) | 15 years | **6.67%** | §8 Abs. 3 EStG 1988 |
| Patents and licences | Contractual or useful life | Varies | |

### Halbjahresabschreibung (Half-Year Rule) — §7 Abs. 2 EStG 1988

| Parameter | Value |
|---|---|
| Rule | In the year of acquisition, only **50%** of the annual depreciation is allowed (half-year convention) |
| Exception | If the asset is acquired in the first half of the year, the half-year rule still applies |
| Disposal year | No depreciation in the year of disposal (full year in the preceding year) |

### Low-Value Assets (Geringwertige Wirtschaftsgüter — GWG) — §13 EStG 1988

| Parameter | Value |
|---|---|
| Immediate expensing threshold | Assets with acquisition cost ≤ **€1,000** (from 2023; previously €800) |

### Accelerated Depreciation — §7 Abs. 1a EStG 1988

| Parameter | Value |
|---|---|
| Declining balance | Up to **30%** of declining book value per year |
| Eligible | New tangible fixed assets acquired after 1 July 2020 |
| Excluded | Buildings, used assets, goodwill, intangible assets |

## 2. Business Operating Expenses

### General Deductibility — §4 Abs. 4 EStG 1988 / §7 Abs. 2 KStG 1988

Business expenses (Betriebsausgaben) are deductible if caused by the business operations.

### Key Deductible Operating Expenses

| Expense Category | Deductibility | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Employee salaries and wages | Fully deductible | Must be arm's-length for related parties |
| Employer social insurance contributions | Fully deductible | Health, pension, accident, unemployment |
| Rent and lease payments | Fully deductible | |
| Professional fees | Fully deductible | |
| Repairs and maintenance | Deductible if revenue nature | Capital improvements must be depreciated |
| Bad debts | Deductible when specifically provided for or written off | |
| Insurance premiums | Fully deductible | |
| Training and education | Fully deductible | |
| Travel expenses | Deductible at actual cost or official flat rates | |
| Start-up costs | Deductible in year incurred | |

### Meals and Entertainment — §20 Abs. 1 Z 3 EStG 1988

| Expense Type | Deductibility |
|---|---|
| Business meals (with clients) | **50%** deductible |
| Staff meals (canteen) | Fully deductible |
| Entertainment (purely social) | **Non-deductible** |
| Internal company events | Deductible up to €365 per employee per year |

### Charitable Donations — §4a EStG 1988 / §6 Abs. 1 Z 3 KStG 1988 [unverified — confirm with local counsel]

| Parameter | Value |
|---|---|
| Deductible | Donations to qualifying public-benefit entities, research institutions, universities |
| Cap | **10%** of prior-year taxable income |
| Eligible recipients | Must be listed on BMF register of qualifying organisations |

## 3. Interest & Financing Costs

### ATAD Interest Limitation — §12a KStG 1988 (from 1 January 2021)

| Parameter | Value |
|---|---|
| Deductible limit | Net interest expense (Zinsüberhang) up to **30%** of tax-EBITDA |
| De minimis | **€3,000,000** net interest expense (Freibetrag — if under, fully deductible) |
| Standalone clause | Entities not part of a consolidated group may deduct fully (subject to anti-abuse review) |
| Equity escape clause | Unlimited deduction if entity's equity ratio ≥ group equity ratio (tolerance of 2 percentage points) |
| Carry-forward | Disallowed interest carried forward **indefinitely** |
| EBITDA carry-forward | Unused EBITDA carried forward **5 years** |

### General Interest Rules

| Parameter | Value |
|---|---|
| Arm's-length | Interest on related-party loans must be at arm's-length rates |
| Hidden profit distribution | Excessive interest to shareholders recharacterised as dividend — §8 Abs. 2 KStG 1988 |

## 4. Research & Development

Austria provides a **14%** research premium (Forschungsprämie) on qualifying R&D expenditure under §108c EStG 1988. The premium is a cash refund (tax-free and independent of taxable income), making it particularly valuable for loss-making companies. Qualifying expenditure includes in-house R&D personnel costs, materials, and contracted R&D (limited to costs of domestic subcontractors).

See [R&D Incentives](rnd_incentives.md) for full program detail.

## 5. Loss Carry-Forward & Carry-Back

### Loss Carry-Forward — §8 Abs. 4 Z 2 KStG 1988

| Parameter | Value |
|---|---|
| Duration | **Indefinite** |
| Restriction | Losses offset up to **75%** of taxable income per year |
| Minimum taxable income | 25% of annual income always taxable (Mindestbesteuerung) |

### Loss Carry-Back

| Parameter | Value |
|---|---|
| Status | **Not available** under Austrian tax law (a temporary COVID-19 carry-back was available but has expired) |

### Group Taxation (Gruppenbesteuerung) — §9 KStG 1988

| Parameter | Value |
|---|---|
| Mechanism | Group members' results (profits and losses) are pooled at the group parent level |
| Eligible | Austrian parent holding ≥50% of capital and voting rights in group members (directly or indirectly) |
| Foreign subsidiaries | Losses of qualifying foreign subsidiaries attributable to Austrian parent may be included (subject to recapture when foreign subsidiary becomes profitable) |
| Minimum period | **3 years** |

### Change of Ownership

| Parameter | Value |
|---|---|
| Mantelkauf (shell acquisition) | Losses forfeited if ≥75% change in ownership combined with change in organisational and economic structure — §8 Abs. 4 Z 2 KStG 1988 |

## 6. Provisions & Reserves

### Tax-Deductible Provisions (Rückstellungen) — §9 EStG 1988

| Provision Type | Deductibility | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Provisions for uncertain liabilities | Deductible | Must be probable and estimable — §9 Abs. 1 Z 1 EStG 1988 |
| Warranty provisions | Deductible | Based on historical experience |
| Litigation provisions | Deductible | If proceedings are pending |
| Environmental / decommissioning | Deductible | If legal obligation exists |
| Employee severance (Abfertigung alt — pre-2003 system) | Deductible provision | §14 EStG 1988 (old system) |
| Employee severance (Abfertigung neu — post-2003) | Contributions to external fund (MVK) deductible | BMSVG |
| Anniversary bonus provisions | Deductible | §14 EStG 1988 |

### Restrictions on Provisions

| Restriction | Detail |
|---|---|
| Discounting | Long-term provisions must be discounted at **3.5%** — §9 Abs. 5 EStG 1988 [unverified — confirm with local counsel] |
| Provisions for future expenses | **Not deductible** unless specific legal or contractual obligation |
| General reserves | **Not deductible** |

## 8. Treaties & Transfer Pricing

### Transfer Pricing — §6 Z 6 EStG 1988 / Verrechnungspreisrichtlinien 2021

| Parameter | Detail |
|---|---|
| Standard | Arm's-length principle — §6 Z 6 EStG 1988 (cross-border), Austrian TP Guidelines 2021 |
| Documentation | Master file and local file required for qualifying entities — Verrechnungspreisdokumentationsgesetz (VPDG) |
| Thresholds | Revenue ≥ €50M or qualifying international group membership |
| Country-by-Country Reporting | Required for MNE groups with consolidated revenue ≥ €750M — VPDG §3 |
| Penalties | Up to €50,000 for failure to file CbCR; general penalties for documentation deficiencies |
| APAs | Bilateral APAs available through BMF |

### Participation Exemption (International) — §10 KStG 1988

| Parameter | Value |
|---|---|
| Dividends from domestic subsidiaries | **100%** exempt (inter-company privilege — Schachtelprivileg) |
| Dividends from EU/EEA subsidiaries | **100%** exempt if ≥10% holding for ≥1 year |
| Dividends from non-EU subsidiaries | **100%** exempt if ≥10% holding for ≥1 year and subsidiary subject to comparable tax |
| Capital gains on international participations | Option to elect taxation or exemption (election must be made upfront and is irrevocable for 10 years) — §10 Abs. 3 KStG 1988 |

### Treaty Network

Austria has over **90** double taxation agreements.

| Income Type | Typical Treaty Rate |
|---|---|
| Dividends (substantial holding) | 0–5% |
| Dividends (portfolio) | 15% |
| Interest | 0–10% |
| Royalties | 0–10% |
