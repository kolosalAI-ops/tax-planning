# Japan - Taxable Income Deductions

> **Jurisdiction**: National (National Tax Agency — NTA)
> **Governing Law**: Corporation Tax Act (法人税法 — CTA); Special Taxation Measures Law (租税特別措置法 — STML)
> **Tax Year**: 2024/2025
> **Currency**: JPY (¥)

---

## 1. Depreciation & Capital Allowances

### 1.1 General Depreciation Methods

> **Legal Basis**: CTA Art. 31; Ministry of Finance Ordinance on Useful Lives of Depreciable Assets (耐用年数省令)

| Method | Description | Applicability |
|---|---|---|
| **Declining Balance (定率法)** | Accelerated method; default for tangible assets (except buildings) | Machinery, vehicles, furniture |
| **Straight-Line (定額法)** | Equal annual deductions over useful life | Buildings (mandatory post-April 2007); intangibles |

Buildings, building improvements, and structures acquired on or after 1 April 2016 must use the straight-line method.

#### Key Asset Useful Lives (Ministry of Finance Ordinance)

| Asset Category | Useful Life | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| **Reinforced concrete buildings (office)** | **50 years** | SL only |
| **Reinforced concrete buildings (factory)** | **38 years** | SL only |
| **Steel-frame buildings (office)** | **38 years** | SL only |
| **Wooden buildings** | **22–24 years** | SL only |
| **Building improvements** | **10–18 years** | SL only (post-April 2016) |
| **Structures** | **10–50 years** | SL only (post-April 2016) |
| **General machinery & equipment** | **5–15 years** | DB or SL |
| **Motor vehicles (passenger)** | **6 years** | DB or SL |
| **Trucks** | **4–5 years** | DB or SL |
| **Computers** | **4 years** | DB or SL |
| **Software (purchased)** | **5 years** | SL |
| **Software (in-house developed)** | **3 years** | SL |
| **Furniture & fixtures** | **5–15 years** | DB or SL |
| **Patents** | **8 years** | SL |
| **Trademarks** | **10 years** | SL |
| **Goodwill** | **5 years** | SL (tax); up to 60 months per CTA Art. 32 |

### 1.2 Immediate Expensing for SMEs

> **Legal Basis**: STML §67-5

| Parameter | Value |
|---|---|
| Eligible companies | Blue-return filing SMEs with capital ≤ JPY 100 million |
| Threshold per asset | **JPY 300,000** or less — immediate expensing |
| Aggregate limit | **JPY 3 million** per year |
| Collective depreciation | Assets JPY 100,000–JPY 200,000 may be pooled and depreciated over **3 years** (均等償却) |

### 1.3 Special Depreciation / Accelerated Write-Off

> **Legal Basis**: STML §42–§68 (various provisions)

| Incentive | Rate | Legal Basis |
|---|---|---|
| **Digital transformation (DX) investment** | 30% special depreciation or 3% tax credit | STML §42-12-7 |
| **Carbon-neutral / green investment** | 50% special depreciation or 5–10% tax credit | STML §42-12-7 |
| **Productivity improvement (SMEs)** | 30% special depreciation or 7% tax credit | STML §42-6 |
| **Regional revitalisation investment** | 15–25% special depreciation | STML §42-11-2 |

## 2. Business Operating Expenses

### 2.1 General Deductibility

> **Legal Basis**: CTA Art. 22 (general expenses deduction)

| Expense Category | Deductibility | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Salaries, wages, bonuses | **100%** | Must be reasonable; excess director compensation may be disallowed |
| Social insurance (employer share) | **100%** | Health insurance (~5%), pension (~9.15%), employment insurance (~0.6%) |
| Rent and lease payments | **100%** | Operating leases |
| Repairs and maintenance | **100%** | Revenue nature; capital improvements capitalised |
| Insurance premiums | **100%** | Business-related |
| Travel expenses | **100%** | Business-related; reasonable amounts |
| Advertising and marketing | **100%** | |
| Bad debts (specific write-off) | **100%** | Must meet specific criteria (CTA Art. 52) |
| Legal and professional fees | **100%** | Revenue nature |
| Training expenses | **100%** | Job-related |

### 2.2 Director Compensation

> **Legal Basis**: CTA Art. 34–36

| Type | Deductibility | Conditions |
|---|---|---|
| Regular monthly compensation (定期同額給与) | **Deductible** | Must be fixed amount, same each month — CTA Art. 34(1)(i) |
| Pre-determined compensation (事前確定届出給与) | **Deductible** | Must be notified to tax office in advance — CTA Art. 34(1)(ii) |
| Profit-linked compensation (利益連動給与) | **Deductible** | Listed companies only; algorithm disclosed — CTA Art. 34(1)(iii) |
| Excessive compensation | **Non-deductible** | Unreasonable portion disallowed — CTA Art. 34(2) |
| Retirement allowance (役員退職金) | **Deductible** | If reasonable amount; formula: final monthly pay x years x factor |

### 2.3 Entertainment Expenses (交際費)

> **Legal Basis**: STML §61-4

| Company Size | Treatment |
|---|---|
| **Capital ≤ JPY 100 million** | Deductible up to **JPY 8 million** per year OR 50% of dining expenses |
| **Capital > JPY 100 million** | **50%** of dining expenses (meals ≤ JPY 10,000/person) deductible; other entertainment non-deductible |
| **Capital > JPY 10 billion** | All entertainment **non-deductible** (from FY2024, dining ≤ JPY 10,000/person at 50% is allowed) [unverified — confirm with local counsel] |

### 2.4 Charitable Donations (寄付金)

> **Legal Basis**: CTA Art. 37

| Donation Type | Limit |
|---|---|
| Government / designated public interest | **100%** deductible (no limit) |
| Specified public interest corporations | Formula-based limit: (capital x 0.375% + income x 6.25%) x 50% |
| General donations | Formula-based limit: (capital x 0.25% + income x 2.5%) x 25% |
| Related-party donations | Subject to transfer pricing if cross-border |

## 3. Interest & Financing Costs

### 3.1 General Deductibility

> **Legal Basis**: CTA Art. 22

Interest on business borrowings is generally deductible. Related-party interest must be at arm's length.

### 3.2 Earnings Stripping Rules

> **Legal Basis**: STML §66-5-2; CTA Art. 142-10

| Parameter | Value |
|---|---|
| Limitation | Net interest expense to related parties limited to **20%** of adjusted taxable income (EBITDA equivalent) |
| De minimis | Net interest expense ≤ **JPY 20 million** exempt |
| Carry-forward of disallowed interest | **10 years** (extended from 7 years for interest incurred in years beginning 1 April 2022 onward) |
| Related parties | Includes overseas controlling shareholders (≥ 50% direct/indirect) and third-party back-to-back arrangements |
| Adjusted taxable income | Taxable income + depreciation + net related-party interest |

### 3.3 Thin Capitalisation

> **Legal Basis**: STML §66-5

| Parameter | Value |
|---|---|
| Debt-to-equity ratio | **3:1** for related-party overseas debt |
| Excess interest | Non-deductible |
| Application | Applies to interest paid to foreign controlling shareholders |

## 4. Research & Development

Japan provides generous R&D tax credits under the STML. The general R&D credit is 2%–14% of qualifying R&D expenditure (rate varies with R&D intensity ratio), with an additional credit for incremental R&D. SMEs benefit from enhanced rates of 12%–17%. Credits are capped at 25%–30% of corporation tax liability.

See [R&D Incentives](rnd_incentives.md) for full program detail.

## 5. Loss Carry-Forward & Carry-Back

### 5.1 Loss Carry-Forward

> **Legal Basis**: CTA Art. 57

| Parameter | Value |
|---|---|
| Duration | **10 years** |
| Utilisation limit (large companies) | **50%** of taxable income per year (capital > JPY 100 million) |
| Utilisation limit (SMEs) | **100%** of taxable income (capital ≤ JPY 100 million) |
| Requirement | Must file blue-return (青色申告) — CTA Art. 57(1) |
| Continuity | Losses survive corporate reorganisations if qualifying conditions met |

### 5.2 Loss Carry-Back

> **Legal Basis**: CTA Art. 80

| Parameter | Value |
|---|---|
| Duration | **1 year** carry-back |
| Availability | SMEs (capital ≤ JPY 100 million) and liquidating companies only |
| Large companies | Carry-back **suspended** (STML §66-13); not available |
| Mechanism | Refund of prior year tax paid |
| Requirement | Blue-return filing |

## 6. Provisions & Reserves

> **Legal Basis**: CTA Art. 52–56

| Provision Type | Deductibility | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Allowance for doubtful accounts (貸倒引当金) | **Deductible** | SMEs and certain financial institutions; historical bad-debt ratio method or individual assessment — CTA Art. 52 |
| Product warranty reserves | **Not deductible** | Except for specific statutory reserves |
| Retirement benefit reserves (退職給付引当金) | **Not deductible** as provision | Deductible when actually paid |
| Bonus accruals (賞与引当金) | **Deductible** | If paid within 1 month after fiscal year-end and accrued — CTA Art. 54 |
| Return reserve (返品調整引当金) | **Abolished** | Transitional measures ended |

Large corporations (capital > JPY 100 million) are generally not allowed the allowance for doubtful accounts except for specific individual assessment items.

## 7. Industry-Specific Deductions

### 7.1 SME Investment Promotion

> **Legal Basis**: STML §42-6

| Parameter | Value |
|---|---|
| Eligible | Blue-return SMEs (capital ≤ JPY 100 million) |
| Qualifying assets | Machinery ≥ JPY 1.6 million, certain software ≥ JPY 700,000 |
| Benefit | 30% special depreciation or 7% tax credit (capped at 20% of tax liability) |

### 7.2 National Strategic Sectors

> **Legal Basis**: STML (various provisions updated annually)

- Semiconductor manufacturing: special depreciation / tax credits
- Battery / EV manufacturing: enhanced credits
- Data centre investment in regional areas: accelerated depreciation

### 7.3 Anti-Tax Haven (CFC) Rules

> **Legal Basis**: STML §66-6–§66-9-4

Profits of controlled foreign companies in low-tax jurisdictions (effective rate < 20%) may be attributed to Japanese parent companies. This affects the deductibility of payments to such subsidiaries.

## 8. Treaties & Transfer Pricing

### 8.1 Transfer Pricing

> **Legal Basis**: STML §66-4; CTA Art. 132

| Parameter | Value |
|---|---|
| Arm's length standard | Mandatory for all related-party cross-border transactions |
| Methods | CUP, resale price, cost plus, TNMM, profit split, and other methods |
| Documentation | Contemporaneous documentation required; local file + master file |
| CbC reporting | Required for groups with consolidated revenue ≥ JPY 100 billion |
| Penalties | Understatement penalties (10%–15%); additional penalties for non-documentation |
| APA | Advance Pricing Arrangements available (unilateral, bilateral, multilateral) |

### 8.2 Treaty Network

Japan has over 80 tax treaties covering more than 150 jurisdictions. Japan adopted the MLI with effect from 2019.
