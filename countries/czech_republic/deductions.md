# Czech Republic - Taxable Income Deductions

> **Jurisdiction**: National (Financni sprava — Financial Administration)
> **Governing Law**: Zakon o danich z prijmu c. 586/1992 Sb. (ZDP) — Income Tax Act
> **Tax Year**: 2024/2025
> **Currency**: CZK (Kc)

---

## 1. Depreciation & Capital Allowances

Czech tax depreciation is governed by ZDP §26-33. Assets with acquisition cost exceeding CZK 80,000 and useful life exceeding 1 year must be depreciated. The taxpayer assigns each asset to one of six depreciation groups.

### 1.1 Depreciation Groups — ZDP §30

| Group | Useful Life | Example Assets | Statutory Basis |
|---|---|---|---|
| **1** | 3 years | Computers, office equipment, tools | ZDP §30 (1) |
| **2** | 5 years | Passenger cars, trucks, machinery, furniture | ZDP §30 (1) |
| **3** | 10 years | Metal structures, turbines, generators | ZDP §30 (1) |
| **4** | 20 years | Pipelines, transmission towers, fencing | ZDP §30 (1) |
| **5** | 30 years | Buildings (industrial, commercial), roads | ZDP §30 (1) |
| **6** | 50 years | Administrative/office buildings, hotels, residential | ZDP §30 (1) |

### 1.2 Depreciation Methods — ZDP §31-32

**Straight-Line (rovnomerne) — ZDP §31:**

| Group | Year 1 Rate | Subsequent Years Rate |
|---|---|---|
| **1** | 20% | 40% |
| **2** | 11% | 22.25% |
| **3** | 5.5% | 10.5% |
| **4** | 2.15% | 5.15% |
| **5** | 1.4% | 3.4% |
| **6** | 1.02% | 2.02% |

**Declining Balance (zrychlene) — ZDP §32:**

| Group | Year 1 Coefficient | Subsequent Years Coefficient |
|---|---|---|
| **1** | 3 | 4 |
| **2** | 5 | 6 |
| **3** | 10 | 11 |
| **4** | 20 | 21 |
| **5** | 30 | 31 |
| **6** | 50 | 51 |

**Note:** Under declining balance, Year 1 depreciation = cost / Year 1 coefficient; subsequent years = (2 x residual value) / (subsequent coefficient - years already depreciated).

### 1.3 Increased First-Year Depreciation — ZDP §31 (1a); §32 (2a)

| Category | Increase | Conditions |
|---|---|---|
| **First owner of Group 1-3 assets** | +20% (straight-line) or increased coefficient | Must be first registered owner — ZDP §31 (1a) [unverified — confirm with local counsel] |
| **Agricultural assets** | +20% | Qualifying agricultural and water management equipment |
| **Environmental equipment** | +15% | Qualifying environmental protection equipment |

### 1.4 Intangible Assets — ZDP §32a

| Asset Type | Amortization Period | Statutory Basis |
|---|---|---|
| **Software (acquired)** | Minimum 36 months (or per license term) | ZDP §32a (4) [unverified — confirm with local counsel — 2021 reform may have removed statutory minimum] |
| **Patents, industrial rights** | Per useful life or contract term | ZDP §32a |
| **Goodwill** | 180 months (15 years) | ZDP §32a (5) |
| **Know-how** | Per contract term, minimum 36 months | ZDP §32a |

**Note:** From 2021, the obligation to amortize intangibles over minimum statutory periods has been relaxed; intangible assets may follow accounting amortization for tax purposes [unverified — confirm with local counsel].

### 1.5 Emission-Free Vehicles — ZDP §30a (from 2024)

| Parameter | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Accelerated period** | 24 months — ZDP §30a |
| **Applicable period** | Vehicles placed in service 2024-2028 |
| **Method** | Monthly depreciation (100% over 24 months) |

### 1.6 Passenger Car Depreciation Cap (from 2024)

| Parameter | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Cap** | Maximum depreciable acquisition cost: CZK 2,000,000 — ZDP §29 (10) (effective from 1 January 2024) |
| **Excess** | Depreciation on amount exceeding CZK 2M is non-deductible |

### 1.7 Low-Value Assets

| Parameter | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Threshold** | Assets with acquisition cost ≤CZK 80,000 may be fully expensed in the year of acquisition |
| **Classification** | Not classified as long-term tangible assets |

## 2. Business Operating Expenses

General deductibility is governed by ZDP §24 — expenses (costs) incurred for the purpose of achieving, securing, and maintaining taxable income are deductible.

### 2.1 Fully Deductible Expenses — ZDP §24

| Expense Category | Deductibility | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| **Employee salaries & wages** | 100% | Including bonuses |
| **Employer social security & health insurance** | 100% | 33.8% employer rate (24.8% social + 9% health) |
| **Rent (business premises)** | 100% | Must be business use |
| **Insurance premiums** | 100% | Business-related risks |
| **Professional fees** | 100% | Legal, audit, consulting |
| **Advertising & marketing** | 100% | Business purpose required |
| **Travel expenses** | 100% | Business travel; per diem rates set by Ministry of Labour |
| **Training expenses** | 100% | Job-related employee training — ZDP §24 (2) j (3) |
| **Bad debts (realized)** | 100% | Must meet conditions of ZDP §24 (2) y |

### 2.2 Partially Deductible / Restricted

| Expense Category | Limit | Statutory Basis |
|---|---|---|
| **Entertainment / representation** | 50% deductible (effective from 2024 reform [unverified — confirm with local counsel]) | ZDP §25 (1) t |
| **Meals & refreshments for employees** | Deductible up to 70% of per diem rate | ZDP §24 (2) j (4) |
| **Charitable donations** | Deductible up to 10% of taxable income (up to 30% until 2025 under COVID extension [unverified — confirm with local counsel]) | ZDP §20 (8) |
| **Passenger car operating costs** | Deductible; no specific cap on operating costs, but mileage-based option available | ZDP §24 (2) k |
| **Pension/life insurance contributions** | Employer contributions deductible up to CZK 50,000 per employee per year — ZDP §24 (2) j (5) | ZDP §24 (2) j (5) |

## 3. Interest & Financing Costs

### 3.1 General Deductibility — ZDP §24 (2) zi

Interest expenses are deductible on an accrual basis if incurred for business purposes.

### 3.2 EBITDA-Based Interest Limitation — ZDP §23e

| Parameter | Detail |
|---|---|
| **General rule** | Excess borrowing costs deductible up to 30% of tax EBITDA — ZDP §23e |
| **De minimis** | CZK 80M — excess borrowing costs up to CZK 80M are always deductible — ZDP §23e (3) |
| **Definition** | Excess borrowing costs = deductible borrowing costs minus taxable borrowing income |
| **Carry-forward** | Non-deductible interest may be carried forward for up to 5 years — ZDP §23e (5) |
| **Scope** | Applies to all borrowing costs (related and unrelated party) |
| **Stand-alone entities** | No exemption for stand-alone entities |

### 3.3 Related-Party Loans — ZDP §23 (7)

| Parameter | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Arm's length** | Related-party loans must bear arm's length interest — ZDP §23 (7) |
| **Thin capitalization** | No separate thin capitalization rule (replaced by EBITDA rule) |
| **Safe harbour rate** | Czech National Bank repo rate + specified spread for CZK-denominated loans [unverified — confirm with local counsel] |

## 4. Research & Development

The Czech Republic provides an R&D tax deduction (odpocet na podporu vyzkumu a vyvoje) under ZDP §34 (4)-(5), allowing companies to deduct 100% of qualifying R&D costs as a standard business expense plus an additional 100% as a special deduction, effectively providing a 200% total deduction. The additional deduction may be increased by 10% for year-over-year increases in R&D spending [unverified — confirm with local counsel].

See [R&D Incentives](rnd_incentives.md) for full program detail.

## 5. Loss Carry-Forward & Carry-Back

### 5.1 Loss Carry-Forward — ZDP §34 (1)

| Parameter | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Duration** | **5 years** — ZDP §34 (1) |
| **Annual cap** | **No cap** — 100% of taxable income may be offset in any year |
| **Ownership change** | Losses may be restricted on substantial change of ownership (>25%) if the nature of business also changes — ZDP §38na |
| **Merger** | Losses may transfer in qualifying mergers subject to conditions — ZDP §23d |

### 5.2 Loss Carry-Back

| Parameter | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Carry-back** | **Not permitted** under Czech law (temporary COVID-era carry-back of 2 years for 2020/2021 losses has expired) |

### 5.3 Group Taxation

| Parameter | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Group relief** | No group taxation regime in Czech Republic — each entity files separately |
| **Group contribution** | Not available — each entity's losses are ring-fenced |

## 6. Provisions & Reserves

### 6.1 Tax-Deductible Reserves — Zakon o rezervach (593/1992 Sb.)

| Reserve Type | Detail | Statutory Basis |
|---|---|---|
| **Repair reserve** | Reserves for future repairs of tangible fixed assets; amounts and timing regulated | Zakon o rezervach §7 |
| **Banking reserves** | Provisions for loan losses by financial institutions | Zakon o rezervach §5 |
| **Insurance reserves** | Technical provisions by insurance companies | Zakon o rezervach §6 |
| **Environmental clean-up** | Reserves for mine reclamation and environmental restoration | Zakon o rezervach §10 |

### 6.2 Repair Reserve Rules — Zakon o rezervach §7

| Parameter | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Eligible assets** | Tangible fixed assets in Groups 2-6 (useful life ≥5 years) |
| **Maximum period** | Reserve must be used within the planned number of years (max equal to useful life of the group) |
| **Bank deposit** | Reserve amounts must be deposited in a dedicated bank account by tax filing deadline — Zakon o rezervach §7 (4) |
| **Non-use** | Unused reserves must be reversed into income |

### 6.3 Bad Debt Provisions — Zakon o rezervach §8a

| Parameter | Detail |
|---|---|
| **General rule** | Tax-deductible provisions for receivables overdue >18 months: up to 50%; overdue >30 months: up to 100% — Zakon o rezervach §8a |
| **Threshold** | Receivable must have been included in income and not exceed CZK 200,000 for simplified provisions |
| **Larger receivables** | Receivables >CZK 200,000 require active enforcement (court proceedings initiated) — Zakon o rezervach §8a (3) |
| **Related-party debts** | Not eligible for tax-deductible provisions — Zakon o rezervach §8a (1) |

### 6.4 Inventory Valuation

| Parameter | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Method** | Cost (FIFO, weighted average, or specific identification) |
| **Write-down** | Provisions for slow-moving/obsolete inventory not directly tax-deductible; only upon actual disposal |

## 8. Treaties & Transfer Pricing

### 8.1 Transfer Pricing — ZDP §23 (7)

| Parameter | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Arm's length principle** | Mandatory for all related-party transactions — ZDP §23 (7) |
| **Documentation** | Contemporaneous TP documentation required (no statutory format; OECD guidelines followed) |
| **Country-by-Country Reporting** | Required for groups with consolidated revenue ≥ CZK 19Bn (approximately €750M) — ZDP §38da |
| **Master File / Local File** | Recommended following OECD BEPS Action 13 guidelines (not strictly mandatory but expected) |
| **Penalties** | Tax surcharge of 20% on adjustments; 1% per month penalty interest on additional tax — Danovy rad §§251-252 |

### 8.2 Accepted Methods

1. Comparable Uncontrolled Price (CUP)
2. Resale Price Method
3. Cost Plus Method
4. Transactional Net Margin Method (TNMM)
5. Profit Split Method

### 8.3 Tax Treaties

| Parameter | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Treaty network** | Czech Republic has 90+ double taxation treaties |
| **Model** | Based on OECD Model Tax Convention |
| **MAP** | Mutual Agreement Procedure available |
| **EU Arbitration Convention** | Applicable for intra-EU disputes |
| **MLI** | Czech Republic has ratified the Multilateral Instrument (MLI) |

### 8.4 Withholding Tax (Outbound)

| Payment Type | Domestic Rate | Treaty-Reduced Rate |
|---|---|---|
| **Dividends** | 15% (or 0% under EU Parent-Subsidiary Directive) | Typically 5-15% |
| **Interest** | 15% | Typically 0-10% |
| **Royalties** | 15% | Typically 0-10% |

## 9. Non-Deductible Items

### 9.1 Expressly Non-Deductible — ZDP §25

| Expense | Reason | Statutory Basis |
|---|---|---|
| **Fines and penalties (imposed by authorities)** | Not incurred for business purposes | ZDP §25 (1) f |
| **Contractual penalties and default interest** | Not deductible if not paid | ZDP §25 (1) z |
| **Income taxes (CIT and foreign equivalents)** | Not a deductible expense | ZDP §25 (1) s |
| **Dividends paid** | Distribution of profits | N/A |
| **50% of entertainment expenses** | Statutory cap | ZDP §25 (1) t [unverified — confirm with local counsel] |
| **Excessive depreciation** | Depreciation beyond statutory rates | ZDP §25 (1) a |
| **Depreciation above CZK 2M passenger car cap** | Non-deductible from 2024 | ZDP §25 (1) a; §29 (10) |
| **Private/personal expenses** | Not business-related | ZDP §25 (1) u |
| **Shortages and losses above insurance proceeds** | Non-deductible to the extent not covered by insurance | ZDP §25 (1) n |
| **Gifts and donations (beyond 10% limit)** | Capped deductibility | ZDP §20 (8) |
| **Provisions not deductible under Reserves Act** | Only reserves per Zakon o rezervach are deductible | ZDP §25 (1) i |
| **Excess borrowing costs** | Beyond EBITDA limit | ZDP §23e |
| **Related-party bad debt provisions** | Not eligible | Zakon o rezervach §8a (1) |
