> **Governing Law**: RIR/2018 (Decreto 9.580/2018), Lei 9.249/1995, Lei 11.196/2005
> **Tax Year**: 2024/2025
> **Currency**: Brazilian Real (BRL / R$)
> **Applies to**: Lucro Real regime (Actual Profit)
> **Additional Legal References**: Lei No. 12.249/2010 (thin capitalization), Lei No. 14.596/2023 (transfer pricing), Lei No. 12.973/2014 (goodwill), Lei No. 6.404/1976 (corporate law / asset valuation), IN RFB No. 1.700/2017 (depreciation rates)

## 2. Loss Carry-Forward (Prejuizo Fiscal)

> **Legal Basis**: RIR/2018, Artigos 320-322 (NOL carry-forward rules: indefinite period, 30% annual cap)

| Parameter | Value | Legal Reference |
|---|---|---|
| Carry-forward period | **Indefinite** | **RIR/2018, Artigo 320** |
| Carry-back | **Not allowed** | **RIR/2018, Artigo 320** (no carry-back provision) |
| Annual limitation | **30%** of taxable income per year | **RIR/2018, Artigo 321** |
| CSLL losses | Same rules - 30% of CSLL base per year | **RIR/2018, Artigo 322; Lei No. 8.981/1995, Artigo 42** |
| Merger/acquisition | Losses of merged company **cannot** be used by surviving entity | **RIR/2018, Artigo 320, SS4o** |
| Spin-off | Losses reduced proportionally to net assets transferred | **RIR/2018, Artigo 320, SS5o** |

**Example**: Company has R$1M taxable income and R$2M accumulated losses:
- Maximum offset: 30% × R$1M = R$300,000
- Taxable income after offset: R$700,000
- Remaining carry-forward: R$1.7M

## 3. Interest on Equity (Juros sobre Capital Proprio - JCP)

> **Legal Basis**: Lei No. 9.249/1995, Artigo 9o (JCP deduction rules)

| Parameter | Value | Legal Reference |
|---|---|---|
| Deductibility | **100%** deductible from IRPJ and CSLL base | **Lei No. 9.249/1995, Artigo 9o, caput** |
| Rate limit | TJLP (Taxa de Juros de Longo Prazo) - set by CMN quarterly | **Lei No. 9.249/1995, Artigo 9o, caput** |
| TJLP rate (2024) | ~**6-7%** per annum (varies quarterly) | CMN resolutions (updated quarterly) |
| Base | Applied to shareholder's equity | **Lei No. 9.249/1995, Artigo 9o, caput** |
| Maximum deduction | Greater of: 50% of current year profit (before JCP) or 50% of retained earnings + profit reserves | **Lei No. 9.249/1995, Artigo 9o, SS1o** |
| WHT on payment | **15%** (or 25% to tax haven residents) | **Lei No. 9.249/1995, Artigo 9o, SS2o; RIR/2018, Artigo 726** |
| Net benefit | ~19% effective deduction (34% - 15% WHT = 19% tax saving) | Effective combined benefit |

> **Note**: Proposed legislation (PL 3394/2024) may limit or eliminate JCP deduction in upcoming reform.

## 4. R&D Deductions - Lei do Bem (Lei 11.196/2005)

| Incentive | Value | Legal Reference |
|---|---|---|

| Maximum combined extra deduction | **80%** of R&D expenditures | **Lei No. 11.196/2005, Artigo 19, SS3o** |
| Accelerated depreciation on R&D equipment | **100%** in year of acquisition | **Lei No. 11.196/2005, Artigo 17, III** |
| Accelerated amortization on R&D intangibles | **100%** in year of acquisition | **Lei No. 11.196/2005, Artigo 17, IV** |
| IRPJ reduction on technology royalty payments | **50%** reduction for IPI on R&D equipment | **Lei No. 11.196/2005, Artigo 17, V** |
| Eligibility | Must be under Lucro Real regime and profitable | **Lei No. 11.196/2005, Artigo 19, caput** |

> **Legal Basis**: Lei No. 12.249/2010, Artigos 24-26 (thin capitalization rules; debt-to-equity ratio 2:1)

| Parameter | Value | Legal Reference |
|---|---|---|
| Debt-to-equity ratio | **2:1** (related parties) | **Lei No. 12.249/2010, Artigo 24** |
| Interest deduction denied | On excess debt above 2:1 ratio | **Lei No. 12.249/2010, Artigo 24, SS1o** |
| Related parties abroad in tax havens | **0.3:1** (30% of equity) | **Lei No. 12.249/2010, Artigo 25** |

|---|---|---|

## 7. Transfer Pricing (Lei 14.596/2023 - New Rules)

> **Legal Basis**: Lei No. 14.596/2023 (new transfer pricing rules, arm's length principle, OECD-aligned)

### New OECD-Aligned Framework (effective 2024)

| Parameter | Value | Legal Reference |
|---|---|---|
| Effective date | January 1, 2024 (mandatory) | **Lei No. 14.596/2023, Artigo 46** |

## 9. Goodwill Amortization (Agio)

> **Legal Basis**: Lei No. 12.973/2014, Artigos 20-22 (goodwill amortization rules)

### Deductible Goodwill (in mergers/acquisitions)

| Parameter | Value | Legal Reference |
|---|---|---|
| Basis | Goodwill based on future profitability (agio por expectativa de rentabilidade futura) | **Lei No. 12.973/2014, Artigo 20** |
| Amortization period | **Minimum 60 months** (5 years) at 1/60 per month | **Lei No. 12.973/2014, Artigo 22** |
| Requirement | Must actually merge/incorporate the acquired entity | **Lei No. 12.973/2014, Artigo 21** |
| Pre-2014 transactions | May still use old rules allowing faster amortization | Transitional rules, Lei 12.973/2014, Artigos 64-68 |
| Post-2014 rules (Lei 12.973/2014) | Only goodwill from genuine third-party acquisitions; must have independent valuation | **Lei No. 12.973/2014, Artigo 20, SS1o** |

## 10. Provisions Deductible for Tax

| Provision Type | Deductibility |
|---|---|
| Vacation accrual (férias) | **Deductible** (when paid or at year-end accrual) |
| 13th salary accrual | **Deductible** |
| Bad debts (individual >R$5K with collection efforts, or >R$30K with judicial proceedings, or debts >1 year overdue >R$100) | **Deductible** |
| Warranty provisions (technical) | **Not deductible** (add back) |
| Contingency provisions | **Not deductible** (add back) |
| Environmental provisions | **Not deductible** (add back, until paid) |
