# Denmark - Taxable Income Deductions

> **Jurisdiction**: National (Skattestyrelsen)
> **Governing Law**: Selskabsskatteloven (SEL); Afskrivningsloven (AL); Ligningsloven (LL)
> **Tax Year**: 2024/2025
> **Currency**: DKK (kr)

---

## 1. Depreciation & Capital Allowances

Denmark's tax depreciation rules are set out in Afskrivningsloven (AL). Depreciation is mandatory for tax purposes — the taxpayer must claim depreciation to the extent assets qualify.

### 1.1 Plant & Machinery — AL §5

| Parameter | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Method** | Declining balance — AL §5 |
| **Maximum rate** | 25% per year — AL §5 (3) |
| **Pooling** | All qualifying plant and machinery are pooled (one pool per entity) — AL §5 (1) |
| **Disposal** | Proceeds reduce the pool balance — AL §5 (4) |
| **Negative pool** | Taxed as recapture income — AL §8 |

### 1.2 Special Assets — AL §5C; AL §5D

| Asset Type | Rate | Statutory Basis |
|---|---|---|
| **IT infrastructure and software** | 25% declining balance (pooled) | AL §5 (standard pool) |
| **Ships** | 12% declining balance | AL §5B |
| **Assets with useful life ≤3 years** | 100% immediate expensing | AL §6 |
| **Low-value assets (≤DKK 33,100 for 2024)** | 100% immediate expensing | AL §6 (indexed annually) |
| **Patents and know-how (acquired)** | Straight-line over useful life (max 7 years) | AL §40 (2) |

### 1.3 Buildings — AL §14

| Building Type | Annual Rate | Statutory Basis |
|---|---|---|
| **Commercial/industrial buildings** | 4% straight-line | AL §14 (1) |
| **Office buildings** | Not depreciable (since 2010) | AL §14 (2) — non-qualifying |
| **Residential buildings** | Not depreciable | AL §14 (2) |
| **Installations in buildings** | Depreciated as part of building or separately at 4% | AL §15 (3) |

**Note:** Office buildings, retail premises, and residential buildings are excluded from tax depreciation since the 2010 reform — AL §14 (2). Only buildings used wholly or mainly for manufacturing, workshops, or similar qualifying purposes are depreciable.

### 1.4 Improvement & Repair Costs

| Parameter | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Repairs** | Immediately deductible (revenue expense) — LL §15 |
| **Improvements** | Added to depreciation base of the asset — AL §18 |
| **Distinction test** | Replacement of existing functionality = repair; enhancement = improvement |

## 2. Business Operating Expenses

General deductibility is governed by Statsskatteloven (SL) §6a — expenses incurred for the purpose of acquiring, securing, or maintaining income are deductible.

### 2.1 Fully Deductible Expenses — SL §6a

| Expense Category | Deductibility | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| **Employee salaries & wages** | 100% | Including bonuses, holiday pay |
| **Employer social contributions** | 100% | Danish employers pay limited mandatory contributions (labour market contribution is employee-borne) |
| **Rent (business premises)** | 100% | Must be business use |
| **Insurance premiums** | 100% | Business-related risks |
| **Professional fees** | 100% | Legal, audit, consulting |
| **Advertising & marketing** | 100% | Business purpose required |
| **Travel expenses** | 100% | Business travel; per diem rates apply |
| **Training expenses** | 100% | Job-related employee training |
| **Bad debts (realized)** | 100% | Must be definitively uncollectible — Kursgevinstloven §3 |

### 2.2 Partially Deductible / Restricted

| Expense Category | Limit | Statutory Basis |
|---|---|---|
| **Meals & entertainment** | 25% deductible (75% non-deductible) | LL §8 (4) |
| **Representation** | 25% deductible | LL §8 (4) |
| **Membership fees (trade associations)** | Deductible if business-related | LL §13 |
| **Gifts to business contacts** | DKK 900 per person per year [unverified — confirm with local counsel] | Skattestyrelsen guidance |
| **Pension contributions** | Deductible within statutory limits | Pensionsbeskatningsloven |

## 3. Interest & Financing Costs

### 3.1 General Deductibility — SL §6a; SEL §§11-11C

Interest expenses are generally deductible on an accrual basis. Denmark applies a two-tier interest limitation system.

### 3.2 Thin Capitalization — SEL §11

| Parameter | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Debt-to-equity ratio** | 4:1 threshold — SEL §11 |
| **Scope** | Controlled debt (related-party) only — SEL §11 (1) |
| **De minimis** | DKK 10M in controlled debt — SEL §11 (1) |
| **Effect** | Excess interest non-deductible; no recharacterization as dividend |

### 3.3 EBITDA Test — SEL §11C

| Parameter | Detail |
|---|---|
| **General rule** | Net financing expenses deductible up to 30% of tax EBITDA — SEL §11C (1) |
| **De minimis** | DKK 21.3M — net financing expenses up to DKK 21.3M are always deductible — SEL §11C (2) |
| **Scope** | Applies to all net financing expenses (related and unrelated party) |
| **Carry-forward** | Non-deductible interest cannot be carried forward under this rule |

### 3.4 Asset Test — SEL §11B

| Parameter | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Rule** | Net financing expenses cannot exceed 1.0% of the tax value of certain qualifying assets — SEL §11B (1) [unverified — confirm with local counsel] |
| **Qualifying assets** | Depreciable assets, trade receivables, and other qualifying assets |
| **Combined application** | The asset test and EBITDA test apply independently; interest is denied if either test is breached |

### 3.5 Combined Effect

Both the asset test (§11B) and EBITDA test (§11C) apply independently. Interest expense is non-deductible to the extent it exceeds the lower of the two caps (after applying the DKK 21.3M de minimis) [unverified — the precise interaction mechanics between §11B and §11C should be confirmed with local counsel]. The thin capitalization rule (§11) applies separately and first.

## 4. Research & Development

Denmark provides a 130% super-deduction for qualifying R&D expenditures under Ligningsloven §8B. This means that for every DKK 100 of qualifying R&D expense, the company may deduct DKK 130 from taxable income, resulting in an additional 30% benefit. The enhanced deduction has been made permanent from 2026 [unverified — confirm with local counsel].

See [R&D Incentives](rnd_incentives.md) for full program detail.

## 5. Loss Carry-Forward & Carry-Back

### 5.1 Loss Carry-Forward — SEL §12

| Parameter | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Duration** | **Indefinite** carry-forward — SEL §12 (1) |
| **Annual cap** | Losses may offset up to DKK 8.775M (indexed) of taxable income fully; losses exceeding this threshold offset only 60% of remaining taxable income — SEL §12 (2) |
| **Example** | If taxable income = DKK 20M: first DKK 8.775M fully offset, then 60% of remaining DKK 11.225M = DKK 6.735M; total offset DKK 15.51M |
| **Ownership change** | Losses forfeited on >50% change of control — SEL §12D |
| **Merger/demerger** | Losses may transfer in tax-free reorganizations subject to restrictions — Fusionsskatteloven |

### 5.2 Loss Carry-Back

| Parameter | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Carry-back** | **Not permitted** under Danish law |

### 5.3 Joint Taxation (Sambeskatning) — SEL §§31-31C

| Parameter | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Mandatory** | All Danish group companies must be jointly taxed — SEL §31 |
| **Optional international** | Optional joint taxation of all worldwide subsidiaries — SEL §31A |
| **Effect** | Losses in one company offset profits in another within the same tax year |
| **Administration company** | One entity designated as administration company responsible for group tax compliance — SEL §31 (4) |
| **Lock-in (international)** | If international joint taxation elected, binding for 10 years — SEL §31A (3) |

### 6.1 Bad Debt Provisions — Kursgevinstloven §3-4

| Parameter | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Realized losses** | Fully deductible — Kursgevinstloven §3 |
| **Provisions** | Mark-to-market taxation applies to most financial instruments including receivables — Kursgevinstloven §25 |

### 6.2 Inventory Valuation

| Parameter | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Method** | Lower of cost or net realizable value |
| **Write-down** | Permitted for obsolete or slow-moving inventory |
| **Tax alignment** | Tax valuation generally follows accounting valuation |

### 6.3 Warranty Provisions

| Parameter | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Deductibility** | Provisions for warranty obligations are generally not deductible until realized |
| **Exception** | Specific warranty claims that are virtually certain may be deductible [unverified — confirm with local counsel] |

### 6.4 Environmental Provisions

| Parameter | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Clean-up obligations** | Deductible when the obligation is realized and paid |
| **Provisions** | Not deductible as accruals until payment |

## 8. Treaties & Transfer Pricing

### 8.1 Transfer Pricing — SEL §§8B-8D; Skattekontrolloven §§37-42

| Parameter | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Arm's length principle** | Mandatory for all related-party transactions — SEL §8B (previously LL §2) |
| **Documentation** | Contemporaneous TP documentation required — Skattekontrolloven §§37-39 |
| **Country-by-Country Reporting** | Required for groups with consolidated revenue ≥ DKK 5.6Bn — Skattekontrolloven §42 |
| **Master File / Local File** | Required following OECD BEPS Action 13 guidelines |
| **Penalties** | DKK 250,000 fine per entity per year for non-compliance with documentation requirements — Skattekontrolloven §41 |

### 8.2 Accepted Methods

2. Resale Price Method
3. Cost Plus Method

### 8.4 Withholding Tax (Outbound)

| Payment Type | Domestic Rate | Treaty-Reduced Rate |
|---|---|---|

## 9. Non-Deductible Items

### 9.1 Expressly Non-Deductible — LL §6; SL §6

| Expense | Reason | Statutory Basis |
|---|---|---|
| **Fines and penalties** | Not incurred for business purposes | SL §6 (negative inference) |
| **Bribes and corrupt payments** | Prohibited by law | LL §7N (non-deductibility of illegal payments) |
| **Income taxes (Danish and foreign)** | Not a deductible expense | SL §6 |
| **Dividends paid** | Distribution of profits | N/A |
| **75% of meals and entertainment** | Statutory cap at 25% deduction | LL §8 (4) |
| **Private/personal expenses** | Not business-related | SL §6 |
| **Donations (exceeding cap)** | Donations deductible up to DKK 18,200 (2024) to approved charities — LL §8A; excess non-deductible | LL §8A |
| **Capital losses on qualifying shares** | Participation exemption applies | SEL §13 (1) |
| **Non-business entertainment** | Not incurred for income production | SL §6 |

### 9.2 Participation Exemption — SEL §13

| Parameter | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Qualifying holdings** | Shares representing ≥10% of capital; held by Danish corporate entity — SEL §13 (1) |
| **Group companies** | Dividends between jointly taxed companies are exempt — SEL §13 (1) |
| **Effect** | Capital gains on qualifying subsidiary shares are tax-exempt; losses are non-deductible — Aktieavancebeskatningsloven §8 |
| **Portfolio shares** | Dividends and gains on <10% holdings are generally taxable |
