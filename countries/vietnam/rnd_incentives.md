# Vietnam - R&D Tax Incentives

## 1. Overview

| Feature | Details |
|---|---|
| **Primary Incentive** | High-tech enterprise: 10% CIT for 15 years |
| **CIT Holiday** | 4-year exemption + 9 years at 50% reduction |
| **R&D Fund** | Mandatory/optional allocation of up to 10% pre-tax profit |
| **Governing Authority** | General Department of Taxation (GDT); Ministry of Science and Technology (MOST); Ministry of Finance (MOF) |
| **Key Legislation** | CIT Law No. 14/2008/QH12 (amended 2013, 2014) Art. 13-14; Decree 218/2013/ND-CP; Decree 13/2019/ND-CP |
| **Corporate Tax Rate** | 20% (standard) |

## 2. Detailed Programs

### 2.1 High-Technology Enterprise Incentives - CIT Law Art. 13

**Legal Basis:** CIT Law 2008 (as amended 2013) Article 13; Decree 218/2013/ND-CP

| Parameter | Details |
|---|---|
| **Preferential CIT Rate** | 10% for 15 years (vs. 20% standard) |
| **CIT Exemption** | 4 years from first year of taxable income |
| **CIT Reduction** | 50% for 9 years following exemption period |
| **Effective Rate During Reduction** | 5% (50% of 10%) |
| **Total Incentive Period** | Up to 28 years (15 years preferential + transition) |

**Qualifying Conditions for High-Tech Enterprise:**
- Enterprise on the list of high technologies encouraged by the State
- Revenue from high-tech products/services ≥ 70% of total revenue
- R&D expenditure ≥ 1% of total annual net revenue
- Number of qualified R&D staff meets minimum thresholds
- Certified by MOST as a high-technology enterprise

### 2.2 Software & IT Enterprise Incentives

**Legal Basis:** Decree 13/2019/ND-CP; CIT Law Art. 13

| Parameter | Details |
|---|---|
| **Preferential CIT Rate** | 10% for 15 years |
| **CIT Exemption** | 4 years |
| **CIT Reduction** | 50% for 9 years |
| **Qualifying Activities** | Software production, IT services, digital content |
| **Certification** | MOST certification as software enterprise |

### 2.3 R&D Center Incentives

| Parameter | Details |
|---|---|
| **Preferential CIT Rate** | 10% for 15 years |
| **CIT Exemption** | 4 years |
| **CIT Reduction** | 50% for 9 years |
| **Qualifying Criteria** | Dedicated R&D center with minimum investment and personnel |
| **Minimum Investment** | VND 3 billion in fixed assets for R&D |
| **Minimum Researchers** | At least 10 qualified researchers |

### 2.4 Science & Technology Fund Allocation - CIT Law Art. 14

**Legal Basis:** CIT Law Art. 14; Decree 95/2014/ND-CP; Circular 12/2016/TT-BKHCN

| Parameter | Details |
|---|---|
| **Allocation Rate** | Up to 10% of pre-tax profit |
| **Purpose** | Science and technology development activities |
| **Tax Treatment** | Deductible from taxable income when allocated |
| **Usage Deadline** | Must be used within 5 years of allocation |
| **Non-Use Penalty** | Unused amounts after 5 years must be included in taxable income + interest at 1.5x central bank rate |
| **Qualifying Uses** | R&D projects, technology acquisition, IP development, researcher training |

**Fund Usage Rules:**

| Qualifying Use | Examples |
|---|---|
| **R&D investment** | Equipment, materials, personnel for R&D projects |
| **Technology acquisition** | Purchase of patents, licenses, know-how |
| **Technology renewal** | Upgrading production technology |
| **Training** | Training researchers and technical staff |
| **IP development** | Patent applications, IP protection |

### 2.5 Economic Zone & Industrial Park Incentives

| Zone Type | CIT Rate | Exemption | Reduction |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Economic Zones** | 10% for 15 years | 4 years | 9 years at 50% |
| **High-Tech Parks** | 10% for 15 years | 4 years | 9 years at 50% |
| **Industrial Parks (disadvantaged areas)** | 17% for 10 years | 2 years | 4 years at 50% |
| **Special Economic Zones** | 10% for 30 years | 4 years | 9 years at 50% |

### 2.6 Import Duty Exemptions for R&D

| Parameter | Details |
|---|---|
| **Equipment** | Import duty exempt for R&D equipment not domestically produced |
| **Materials** | Import duty exempt for materials used in R&D (for 5 years) |
| **Samples** | Import duty exempt for R&D samples and prototypes |
| **Legal Basis** | Law on Import and Export Duties; Decree 134/2016/ND-CP |

## 3. Patent Box / IP Regime

Vietnam does not have a formal patent box regime.

| Aspect | Status |
|---|---|
| **Patent Box** | Not available |
| **IP Amortization** | Allocated over useful life (typically 10-20 years for patents) |
| **IP Transfer** | Subject to CIT on gains; may qualify for tech transfer incentives |
| **Royalty WHT** | 10% on payments to non-residents (technology transfer) |
| **Technology Transfer** | Exempt from CIT for transfers from overseas in priority technology areas |
| **Alternative** | High-tech enterprise status provides effective IP income protection at 10% CIT |

**Technology Transfer Incentives:**
- Income from technology transfer in priority areas may be exempt from CIT
- Government-encouraged technology transfers receive streamlined approval
- Technology transfer contracts must be registered with MOST

## 4. Effective Benefit Analysis (per $1M USD R&D Spend)

*Assuming VND/USD exchange rate of 24,500; VND 24.5B equivalent spend; standard CIT 20%*

| Scenario | Effective Rate | Tax Saving (VND) | Tax Saving (USD) |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Standard (20% CIT)** | 20% | VND 4.9B deduction benefit | ~$200,000 |
| **High-Tech Enterprise (10%)** | 10% | Additional VND 2.45B saving | ~$100,000 additional |
| **During 4-Year Exemption** | 0% | Full VND 4.9B saving | ~$200,000 |
| **During 9-Year Reduction** | 5% | VND 3.675B saving | ~$150,000 |
| **R&D Fund (10% of profit)** | Deferred | Up to VND 2.45B timing benefit | Up to ~$100,000 |

**Comprehensive 28-Year Example (High-Tech Enterprise):**

| Period | Years | CIT Rate | Annual Tax on $5M Profit |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Exemption** | 1-4 | 0% | $0 |
| **50% Reduction** | 5-13 | 5% | $250,000 |
| **Preferential Rate** | 14-15 | 10% | $500,000 |
| **Standard Rate** | 16+ | 20% | $1,000,000 |

**Key Insight:** Vietnam's incentives are structured as long-term CIT rate reductions and holidays rather than R&D expenditure-based credits. This makes them most valuable for profitable enterprises with sustained operations.

## 5. Claim Process & Documentation

### 5.1 High-Tech Enterprise Certification

| Step | Action | Timeline |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Meet qualifying criteria (revenue, R&D spend, personnel) | Ongoing operations |
| 2 | Apply to MOST for high-tech enterprise certification | Application with supporting documents |
| 3 | MOST review and site inspection | 30-45 days |
| 4 | MOST issues certification | Valid for specific period |
| 5 | Claim preferential CIT rate on annual tax return | Annual filing deadline |
| 6 | Periodic renewal and compliance reporting | As required |

### 5.2 R&D Fund Documentation

| Step | Action | Timeline |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Board resolution to establish S&T fund | Before allocation |
| 2 | Allocate up to 10% of pre-tax profit | During fiscal year |
| 3 | Report allocation in CIT return | Annual filing |
| 4 | Maintain fund expenditure records | Ongoing |
| 5 | Use fund within 5-year window | Within 5 years |
| 6 | Report unused amounts and pay interest | If applicable |

### 5.3 Documentation Requirements

- **MOST certification:** High-tech enterprise or software enterprise certificate
- **Revenue records:** Demonstrating ≥ 70% from qualifying activities
- **R&D expenditure records:** Showing ≥ 1% of net revenue spent on R&D
- **Personnel records:** Qualified researcher list and qualifications
- **Fund records:** S&T fund allocation, usage, and balance reports
- **Project documentation:** R&D project descriptions and outcomes
- **Financial statements:** Audited by independent auditor
- **Investment certificates:** For zone-based incentives

## 6. Recent Changes & Sunset Provisions

| Change | Effective Date | Details |
|---|---|---|
| **Decree 13/2019** | March 2019 | Updated criteria for IT/software enterprise incentives |
| **Resolution 43/2022** | 2022 | Extended fiscal support measures including R&D-related incentives |
| **Circular 03/2021** | 2021 | Updated R&D fund management and usage rules |
| **Draft CIT Law Amendments** | Under review | Potential alignment with OECD Pillar Two requirements |
| **High-Tech Park Expansion** | 2023-2025 | New high-tech parks in Da Nang, Can Tho with R&D incentives |
| **Digital Transformation** | 2024 | Additional incentives for digital technology R&D |

**Sunset Provisions:**
- High-tech enterprise incentives: No explicit sunset; governed by CIT Law (permanent)
- R&D fund allocation: Permanent feature of CIT Law
- Economic zone incentives: Duration specified per zone establishment decree
- Vietnam is not currently subject to OECD Pillar Two but may adopt similar measures
- Government Resolution 02/2024 signals continued support for technology investment
