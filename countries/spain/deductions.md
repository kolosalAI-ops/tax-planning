# Spain - Taxable Income Deductions

> **Jurisdiction**: National (AEAT — Agencia Estatal de Administración Tributaria)
> **Governing Law**: Ley 27/2014, de 27 de noviembre, del Impuesto sobre Sociedades (LIS)
> **Tax Year**: 2024/2025
> **Currency**: EUR (€)

---

## 1. Depreciation & Capital Allowances

### General Principles — Art. 12 LIS

Depreciation (amortización) is deductible if it corresponds to the effective depreciation of the asset. Three methods are permitted.

### Depreciation Methods — Art. 12.1 LIS

| Method | Description | Statutory Basis |
|---|---|---|
| Official tables (tablas oficiales) | Prescribed maximum and minimum rates per asset type | Art. 12.1.a LIS; Reglamento IS (RD 634/2015) |
| Declining balance | For new tangible assets with useful life ≥3 years; coefficient 1.5–2.5× straight-line rate | Art. 12.1.b LIS |
| Sum-of-digits (números dígitos) | Permitted under specific conditions | Art. 12.1.c LIS |
| Justified plan | Approved custom depreciation plan | Art. 12.1.d LIS |

### Key Depreciation Rates — Reglamento IS Tables

| Asset Category | Maximum Rate | Maximum Useful Life | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| Commercial buildings | **2%** | 68 years | |
| Industrial buildings | **3%** | 34 years | |
| Machinery and equipment | **12%** | 18 years | |
| Tools and moulds | **25%** | 8 years | |
| IT equipment (hardware) | **25%** | 8 years | |
| Software | **33%** | 6 years | |
| Motor vehicles | **16%** | 14 years | |
| Furniture | **10%** | 20 years | |
| Office equipment | **15%** | 13 years | |

### Accelerated Depreciation for SMEs — Art. 12.3 LIS

| Parameter | Value |
|---|---|
| Eligible entities | Entities with net revenue < **€10M** (entidades de reducida dimensión — ERD) |
| Benefit | Depreciation at **2×** normal rate for new tangible and intangible assets |
| Freedom of depreciation | Available for new assets up to €300,000 per year if employment is maintained — Art. 12.3 LIS / Art. 102 LIS |

### Intangible Assets — Art. 12.2 LIS

| Parameter | Value |
|---|---|
| Goodwill (fondo de comercio) | Maximum deduction **5%** per year (1/20th) — Art. 12.2 LIS |
| Other intangibles (definite life) | Depreciated over useful life |
| Other intangibles (indefinite life) | Maximum **5%** per year |

## 2. Business Operating Expenses

### General Deductibility — Art. 10–15 LIS

Expenses are deductible if they are (i) accounted for in the profit and loss statement, (ii) incurred for the purpose of earning taxable income, and (iii) supported by documentation.

### Key Deductible Operating Expenses

| Expense Category | Deductibility | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Employee salaries and wages | Fully deductible | Must be arm's-length for related parties |
| Employer social security contributions | Fully deductible | |
| Rent and lease payments | Fully deductible | |
| Professional fees | Fully deductible | |
| Repairs and maintenance | Fully deductible if revenue nature | Capital improvements must be depreciated |
| Bad debts | Deductible after 6 months past due, or debtor in insolvency | Art. 13 LIS |
| Insurance premiums | Fully deductible | |
| Travel expenses | Deductible if business purpose documented | |
| Start-up and incorporation costs | Deductible in year incurred or amortised over 5 years | |

### Charitable Donations — Art. 20 Ley 49/2002

| Parameter | Value |
|---|---|
| Tax deduction | **35%** of qualifying donations (40% for recurring multi-year donations) |
| Cap | **10%** of taxable base |
| Carry-forward of excess | **10 years** |

## 3. Interest & Financing Costs

### Interest Limitation — Art. 16 LIS (ATAD-Aligned)

| Parameter | Value |
|---|---|
| Deductible limit | Net interest expense up to **30%** of operating profit (beneficio operativo — tax EBITDA) |
| De minimis | **€1,000,000** net interest expense (full deduction if under) |
| Carry-forward of excess interest | **Indefinite** |
| Operating profit carry-forward | Unused operating profit capacity carried forward **5 years** |
| Consolidated group | Group-level computation if tax consolidation applies |

### Additional Interest Restrictions — Art. 15.h LIS

| Parameter | Value |
|---|---|
| Intragroup debt for acquisitions | Interest on intragroup loans used to acquire participations from related parties is **non-deductible** |
| Thin capitalisation | No formal debt/equity ratio rule (abolished in 2012); reliance on 30% EBITDA test and transfer pricing |

## 4. Research & Development

Spain provides generous tax credits for R&D (investigación y desarrollo) and technological innovation (innovación tecnológica) activities under Art. 35 LIS. Additionally, the Patent Box regime (Art. 23 LIS) provides a 60% exemption on qualifying IP income. Spain is one of the few EU countries offering both volume-based R&D credits and a patent box concurrently.

See [R&D Incentives](rnd_incentives.md) for full program detail.

## 5. Loss Carry-Forward & Carry-Back

### Loss Carry-Forward — Art. 26 LIS

| Parameter | Value |
|---|---|
| Duration | **Indefinite** |
| Restriction — general | Losses may offset up to **70%** of taxable base (before loss offset) |
| Restriction — large companies (revenue > €20M) | **50%** of taxable base |
| Restriction — very large companies (revenue > €60M) | **25%** of taxable base |
| Minimum offset | **€1,000,000** always available regardless of revenue thresholds |
| Change of control | Losses forfeited if >50% ownership change and entity was inactive or changed activity — Art. 26.4 LIS |

### Loss Carry-Back

| Parameter | Value |
|---|---|
| Status | **Not available** under Spanish tax law |

## 6. Provisions & Reserves

### Tax-Deductible Provisions — Art. 13–14 LIS

| Provision Type | Deductibility | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Bad debt provisions | Deductible after 6 months past due; debtor in insolvency; or debtor-specific circumstances | Art. 13.1 LIS |
| Bad debts — related party | **Not deductible** | Art. 13.1.3° LIS |
| Bad debts — public entities | **Not deductible** | Art. 13.1.3° LIS |
| Warranty provisions | Deductible if based on historical data | |
| Environmental provisions | Deductible if legal obligation | Art. 14 LIS |
| Restructuring provisions | Generally **not deductible** until costs incurred | |
| Pension commitments | Deductible only if externalised through insurance or pension plan | Art. 14.1 LIS |

### Provisions for Impairment — Art. 13 LIS

| Asset Type | Deductibility |
|---|---|
| Receivables impairment | Deductible per bad debt rules |
| Fixed asset impairment | **Not deductible** (must await disposal) |
| Financial asset impairment (participations) | **Not deductible** — Art. 13.2 LIS |
| Goodwill impairment | **Not deductible** for tax (use 5% annual amortisation instead) |

## 7. Industry-Specific Deductions

### Film and Audiovisual — Art. 36 LIS

| Parameter | Value |
|---|---|
| Spanish productions | **30%** credit on first €1M of expenses; **25%** on excess |
| Foreign productions filmed in Spain | **30%** credit on qualifying spend in Spain (cap €10M per production) [unverified — confirm with local counsel] |
| Minimum Spanish spend | €1M (€200,000 for animation) |

### Shipping (Tonnage Tax) — Art. 113–128 LIS

| Parameter | Value |
|---|---|
| Method | Lump-sum profit based on net tonnage |
| Election | 10-year minimum |
| Eligible | Shipping companies with qualifying vessels |

### Small and Medium Enterprises (ERD) — Art. 101–105 LIS

| Incentive | Value |
|---|---|
| Eligibility | Net revenue < **€10M** |
| Accelerated depreciation | 2× normal rates — Art. 103 LIS |
| Bad debt provision | 1% global provision on debtors (excluding related-party and public debts) — Art. 104 LIS |
| Reduced CIT rate | Historical reduced rate abolished; standard 25% applies from 2016 |
| Freedom of depreciation | Up to €300,000 for new assets if employment maintained |

### Canary Islands Special Zone (ZEC) — L. 19/1994

| Parameter | Value |
|---|---|
| Reduced CIT rate | **4%** on qualifying income |
| Investment deduction (RIC) | Up to **90%** of undistributed profits reinvested in qualifying assets |

### Real Estate Investment (SOCIMI) — L. 11/2009

| Parameter | Value |
|---|---|
| CIT rate | **0%** on qualifying income if ≥80% distributed |
| Special tax | **19%** on undistributed profits |

## 8. Treaties & Transfer Pricing

### Transfer Pricing — Art. 18 LIS

| Parameter | Detail |
|---|---|
| Standard | Arm's-length principle — Art. 18.1 LIS |
| Documentation | Master file and local file required for entities with revenue > €45M or specific related-party transaction thresholds — Art. 13–16 Reglamento IS |
| Country-by-Country Reporting | Required for MNE groups with consolidated revenue ≥ €750M — Art. 14 Reglamento IS |
| Penalties | 1% of undocumented transaction amounts (minimum €1,000, maximum €10,000 per data item) — Art. 18.13 LIS |
| APAs | Unilateral and bilateral available — Art. 18.9 LIS |

### Participation Exemption — Art. 21 LIS

| Parameter | Value |
|---|---|
| Dividends | **95%** exempt (5% taxed as non-deductible expense) |
| Capital gains | **95%** exempt |
| Conditions | ≥5% holding for ≥1 year; subsidiary subject to comparable tax (≥10% nominal rate) |
| From 2021 | 5% non-deductible for dividends/gains from subsidiaries with >€40M participation value |

### Treaty Network

Spain has over **90** double taxation agreements.

| Income Type | Typical Treaty Rate |
|---|---|
| Dividends (substantial holding) | 0–5% |
| Dividends (portfolio) | 10–15% |
| Interest | 0–10% |
| Royalties | 0–10% |
