# Canada - R&D Tax Incentives

## 1. Overview

| Parameter | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Headline Incentive** | Scientific Research & Experimental Development (SR&ED) Tax Incentive Program |
| **Mechanism Type** | Refundable and non-refundable investment tax credit; super-deduction |
| **Headline Rate** | 35% refundable (CCPCs on first C$3M) / 15% non-refundable (others) |
| **Refundable** | Yes — for Canadian-Controlled Private Corporations (CCPCs) at enhanced rate; partially refundable for others |
| **Patent Box** | No federal patent box regime |
| **Legal Basis** | Income Tax Act (ITA) §37 (deduction), §127(9)–§127(11.1) (investment tax credit); Income Tax Regulations (ITR) Part XXIX §2900 |

## 2. Detailed Programs

### 2.1 SR&ED Federal Investment Tax Credit (ITC)

| Parameter | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Legal Basis** | ITA §127(9) definition of "investment tax credit"; §127(10.1) enhanced rate for CCPCs; ITR §2900 |
| **Enhanced Rate (CCPCs)** | 35% of qualifying SR&ED expenditures on the first C$3 million expenditure limit |
| **Refundability (CCPCs)** | 100% refundable on the first C$3 million at 35% rate |
| **General Rate** | 15% of qualifying SR&ED expenditures (non-refundable; carry-forward available) |
| **CCPC Expenditure Limit** | C$3 million; reduced when prior-year taxable income exceeds C$500,000 or taxable capital exceeds C$10 million (§127(10.2)) |
| **Qualifying Expenditures** | Current expenditures: salaries & wages, materials consumed, contract payments (80% of arm's length, 100% of non-arm's length), overhead (proxy method or traditional) |
| **Capital Expenditures** | Not eligible (capital expenditures for SR&ED removed effective 2014) |
| **Carry-Forward** | 20 years forward, 3 years back (ITA §127(9)(c)) |
| **Proxy Method for Overhead** | 55% of salary base (ITA §37(8)(a)(ii)(B)); simplified alternative to tracking actual overhead |

### 2.2 SR&ED Deduction (ITA §37)

| Parameter | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Legal Basis** | ITA §37(1) |
| **Deduction Rate** | 100% of current SR&ED expenditures incurred in Canada |
| **Pool Mechanism** | SR&ED expenditures form a deduction pool; may be deducted in year incurred or carried forward indefinitely |
| **Interaction with ITC** | Deduction pool reduced by ITC claimed in previous year |

### 2.3 Provincial R&D Tax Credits (Stacking)

Provincial credits are in addition to the federal SR&ED credit. Key provinces:

| Province | Rate | Refundable | Legal Basis |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Ontario** | 3.5% (Ontario Innovation Tax Credit — OITC) | Yes (for CCPCs) | Ontario Taxation Act, 2007, §89 |
| **Ontario** | 8% (Ontario Research & Development Tax Credit — ORDTC) | No | Ontario Taxation Act, 2007, §90 |
| **Quebec** | 14% (general) to 30% (SMEs, based on assets) | Yes | Taxation Act (Quebec) §1029.6.0.0.1 et seq. |
| **British Columbia** | 3.5% (BC SR&ED Tax Credit) | Yes (for CCPCs) | Income Tax Act (BC) §4.7 |
| **Alberta** | 8% (Alberta SR&ED Tax Credit) | Yes | Alberta Corporate Tax Act §26.2 |
| **Saskatchewan** | 10% (R&D Tax Credit) | No (non-refundable) | Saskatchewan Income Tax Act §64.2 |
| **Manitoba** | 15% (R&D Tax Credit) | Yes (for SMEs) | Manitoba Income Tax Act §11.8 |
| **New Brunswick** | 15% (R&D Tax Credit) | Yes | New Brunswick Income Tax Act §39.1 |
| **Nova Scotia** | 15% (R&D Tax Credit) | Yes | Nova Scotia Income Tax Act §47 |
| **Atlantic Provinces (general)** | 15% (varies) | Generally yes | Respective provincial income tax statutes |

### 2.4 Combined Federal-Provincial Rates

| Taxpayer Type | Federal Rate | Ontario Rate | Combined Rate |
|---|---|---|---|
| **CCPC (first C$3M)** | 35% (refundable) | 3.5% OITC (refundable) | **38.5%** |
| **CCPC (above C$3M)** | 15% (non-refundable) | 8% ORDTC (non-refundable) | **23%** |
| **Non-CCPC** | 15% (non-refundable) | 8% ORDTC (non-refundable) | **23%** |
| **CCPC in Quebec (SME)** | 35% (refundable) | 30% (refundable) | **65%** |

## 3. Patent Box / IP Regime

**Not applicable.** Canada does not currently have a federal or provincial patent box or IP box regime. There have been policy discussions and proposals (including the 2024 federal budget mention of a potential Canadian patent box), but no legislation has been enacted as of the current date.

| Parameter | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Status** | No patent box regime in force |
| **Policy Discussions** | 2024 Federal Budget mentioned potential exploration of IP box |
| **Alternative** | SR&ED program provides front-end R&D support rather than back-end IP income reduction |

## 4. Effective Benefit Analysis

**Assumptions:** C$1,000,000 (~US$735,000) SR&ED spend by a CCPC in Ontario, within C$3M expenditure limit, profitable entity.

| Component | Calculation | Tax Benefit (C$) |
|---|---|---|
| **Federal ITC (35%, refundable)** | C$1,000,000 × 35% | **C$350,000** |
| **Ontario OITC (3.5%, refundable)** | C$1,000,000 × 3.5% | **C$35,000** |
| **§37 Deduction (net of ITC)** | (C$1,000,000 − C$350,000 − C$35,000) × 26.5% combined CIT rate | **C$163,025** |
| **Total Year 1 Benefit** | C$350,000 + C$35,000 + C$163,025 | **C$548,025** |
| **Effective Benefit Rate** | C$548,025 ÷ C$1,000,000 | **~54.8%** |

**Per US$1,000,000 R&D Spend (at ~0.735 CAD/USD):**

| Component | Tax Benefit (USD Equivalent) |
|---|---|
| **Total Benefit** | ~US$403,000 |
| **Effective Rate on USD spend** | **~40.3%** |

> **Note:** Quebec-based SME CCPCs can achieve combined rates exceeding 65% on qualifying SR&ED expenditures, making Canada one of the most generous R&D tax incentive jurisdictions globally.

## 5. Claim Process & Documentation

### Filing Process

| Step | Detail |
|---|---|
| **1. Identify SR&ED Projects** | Determine which projects meet the three criteria: scientific or technological uncertainty, systematic investigation, and technological advancement |
| **2. Document Concurrently** | CRA expects contemporaneous documentation of technical objectives, hypotheses, procedures, and results |
| **3. Complete Form T661** | "Scientific Research and Experimental Development (SR&ED) Expenditures Claim" — technical and financial information |
| **4. Complete Schedule T2SCH31** | "Investment Tax Credit — Corporations" for federal ITC calculation |
| **5. File Provincial Forms** | Respective provincial schedules (e.g., Ontario CT8, Quebec RD-1) |
| **6. Submit with T2 Return** | All forms filed with the corporate income tax return (T2) |
| **7. CRA Review** | CRA may conduct a scientific review (technical) and/or financial review |

### Key Deadlines

| Item | Deadline |
|---|---|
| **SR&ED Claim Filing** | 12 months after the tax filing due date (i.e., 18 months after fiscal year-end) — ITA §37(11) |
| **Corporate Tax Return (T2)** | 6 months after fiscal year-end |
| **Refund Processing** | CRA target: 120 days for complete claims (60 days for claimant assured) |
| **Pre-Approval** | Not required (self-assessment), but CRA offers Pre-Claim Consultation (PCPC) service |
| **First-Time Claimant Advisory Service** | Voluntary CRA service for new claimants |

### Documentation Requirements (CRA T4088 Guide)

- Project descriptions identifying scientific/technological uncertainties
- Records of hypotheses formulated and tested
- Evidence of systematic investigation (experiments, analyses, prototypes)
- Personnel records and time allocation
- Financial records for all claimed expenditures
- Contracts for subcontracted SR&ED work
- Materials and overhead allocation methodology

## 6. Recent Changes & Sunset Provisions

| Change | Effective Date | Detail |
|---|---|---|
| **Proxy Rate Reduction** | 2014 | Proxy rate for overhead reduced from 65% to 55% |
| **Capital Expenditure Removal** | January 1, 2014 | Capital expenditures no longer qualify for SR&ED ITC or deduction |
| **ITC Rate Reduction (general)** | January 1, 2014 | General rate reduced from 20% to 15% |
| **Contract Payment Reduction** | January 1, 2013 | Arm's-length contract payments inclusion rate reduced from 100% to 80% |
| **2024 Budget Proposals** | Proposed 2024 | Potential increase to expenditure limit from C$3M, and modernization of SR&ED program (under consultation) |
| **Patent Box Exploration** | 2024 | Federal government exploring potential Canadian IP box (no legislation tabled) |
| **Sunset Provisions** | None | SR&ED program is a permanent feature of the ITA; no expiry date |
