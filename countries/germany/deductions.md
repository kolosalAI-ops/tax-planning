# Germany - Taxable Income Deductions

> **Jurisdiction**: Federal (BMF — Bundesministerium der Finanzen)
> **Governing Law**: Einkommensteuergesetz (EStG); Körperschaftsteuergesetz (KStG); Gewerbesteuergesetz (GewStG)
> **Tax Year**: 2024/2025
> **Currency**: EUR (€)

---

## 1. Depreciation & Capital Allowances

Germany uses the term "Absetzung für Abnutzung" (AfA) for tax depreciation. The method and rates are governed by §7 EStG and the BMF official depreciation tables (AfA-Tabellen).

### Standard Depreciation Methods — §7 Abs. 1–2 EStG

| Method | Availability | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Straight-line (linear) | Default method for all assets | §7 Abs. 1 EStG |
| Declining balance (degressiv) | Reintroduced for assets acquired 2020–2024 at **20%** (max 2× straight-line) | §7 Abs. 2 EStG (as amended by Wachstumschancengesetz 2024) [unverified — confirm with local counsel] |

### Key Depreciation Rates — BMF AfA-Tabellen

| Asset Category | Useful Life | Annual Rate (Straight-Line) | Statutory Basis |
|---|---|---|---|
| Commercial buildings (after 31 Dec 2022) | 33 years | **3%** | §7 Abs. 4 Satz 1 Nr. 2 EStG |
| Commercial buildings (before 2023) | 33–50 years | 2–3% | §7 Abs. 4 EStG |
| Residential buildings (after 31 Dec 2022) | 33 years | **3%** | §7 Abs. 4 Satz 1 Nr. 2 EStG |
| Machinery and equipment | 4–10 years | 10–25% | BMF AfA-Tabellen |
| Office equipment / furniture | 13 years | ~7.7% | BMF AfA-Tabellen |
| Computers and software | 3 years | **33.3%** | BMF-Schreiben 22 Feb 2022 (immediate expensing option; 1-year write-off permitted) |
| Motor vehicles | 6 years | ~16.7% | BMF AfA-Tabellen |
| Patents and licences | Contractual term or useful life | Varies | §7 Abs. 1 EStG |

### Low-Value Assets (Geringwertige Wirtschaftsgüter — GWG) — §6 Abs. 2 EStG

| Parameter | Value |
|---|---|
| Immediate expensing threshold | Assets with acquisition cost ≤ **€800** (net) |
| Pool option (Sammelposten) | Assets €250–€1,000: pooled and depreciated over **5 years** at 20% p.a. — §6 Abs. 2a EStG |
| Election | Taxpayer may choose GWG immediate write-off OR pool method (but must apply consistently per year) |

### Special Depreciation — §7g EStG (Investitionsabzugsbetrag)

| Parameter | Value |
|---|---|
| Investment deduction allowance | Up to **50%** of planned acquisition cost (deducted in advance of purchase) |
| Eligible businesses | Profit ≤ €200,000 |
| Maximum deduction | **€200,000** (aggregate) |
| Timeframe | Investment must occur within 3 years after deduction |
| Additional special depreciation | **20%** of acquisition cost in year of acquisition (Sonderabschreibung §7g Abs. 5 EStG) |

## 2. Business Operating Expenses

### General Deductibility — §4 Abs. 4 EStG (Betriebsausgaben)

Business expenses (Betriebsausgaben) are deductible if they are caused by the business operations (betrieblich veranlasst).

### Key Deductible Operating Expenses

| Expense Category | Deductibility | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Employee salaries and wages | Fully deductible | Must be arm's-length for related parties |
| Social security employer contributions | Fully deductible | Health, pension, unemployment, long-term care |
| Rent and lease payments | Fully deductible | Subject to Gewerbesteuer add-back (see §8 GewStG) |
| Professional fees | Fully deductible | |
| Repairs and maintenance | Deductible if revenue expenditure | Capital improvements must be depreciated |
| Insurance premiums | Fully deductible | |
| Travel expenses | Deductible at actual cost or flat rates | Meals during travel: flat rate €14/€28 per day — §9 Abs. 4a EStG |
| Bad debts | Deductible when written off or specifically provided for | |
| Charitable donations | Deductible up to **20%** of income or 0.4% of total revenue + wages + salaries | §9 Abs. 1 Nr. 2 KStG |

### Pre-Formation / Start-Up Costs

| Parameter | Value |
|---|---|
| Organisational costs | Deductible in the year incurred |
| Formation costs (Gründungskosten) | Deductible if borne by the company |

## 3. Interest & Financing Costs

### Zinsschranke (Interest Barrier) — §4h EStG / §8a KStG

| Parameter | Value |
|---|---|
| Deductible limit | Net interest expense up to **30%** of tax-EBITDA |
| De minimis (Freigrenze) | **€3,000,000** net interest expense (if under, full deduction; if over, entire amount subject to 30% limit) |
| Standalone clause | Unlimited deduction if company is not part of a consolidated group — §4h Abs. 2 Satz 1 Buchst. b EStG |
| Escape clause (equity ratio) | Unlimited deduction if equity ratio of the company ≥ group equity ratio (minus 2 percentage points) — §4h Abs. 2 Satz 1 Buchst. c EStG |
| Carry-forward | Disallowed interest carried forward indefinitely (Zinsvortrag) — §4h Abs. 1 Satz 5 EStG |
| EBITDA carry-forward | Unused EBITDA capacity carried forward **5 years** — §4h Abs. 1 Satz 3 EStG |

### Gewerbesteuer (Trade Tax) Add-Backs — §8 GewStG

Certain financing expenses are partially added back to the Gewerbesteuer base:

| Expense | Add-Back Percentage |
|---|---|
| Interest on all debt | **25%** of interest exceeding €200,000 |
| Rent for immovable property | **50%** of 25% (effective 12.5%) |
| Rent for movable property | **20%** of 25% (effective 5%) |
| Leasing payments (immovable) | **50%** of 25% |
| Leasing payments (movable) | **20%** of 25% |
| Licence fees | **25%** (from 2008) |

## 4. Research & Development

Germany provides a tax credit for R&D activities under the Forschungszulagengesetz (FZulG), effective from 1 January 2020. The credit is calculated as a percentage of eligible R&D personnel costs and contracted R&D expenses, credited against the taxpayer's income tax or corporate tax liability.

See [R&D Incentives](rnd_incentives.md) for full program detail.

## 5. Loss Carry-Forward & Carry-Back

### Loss Carry-Forward — §10d Abs. 2 EStG / §8 Abs. 1 KStG

| Parameter | Value |
|---|---|
| Duration | **Indefinite** |
| Restriction (Mindestbesteuerung) | Losses offset freely up to **€1,000,000**; above that, only **60%** of remaining taxable income may be offset — §10d Abs. 2 EStG |
| Gewerbesteuer losses | Same rules apply — §10a GewStG |

### Loss Carry-Back — §10d Abs. 1 EStG

| Parameter | Value |
|---|---|
| Duration | **1 year** |
| Maximum | **€10,000,000** (€20,000,000 for joint assessment of individuals) — §10d Abs. 1 Satz 1 EStG |
| Gewerbesteuer | **No carry-back** for trade tax losses |

### Change of Ownership — §8c KStG

| Threshold | Consequence |
|---|---|
| >50% change in ownership | Losses forfeited entirely (subject to §8d hidden reserves exception) |
| ≤50% change (pre-2024) | Previously proportional forfeiture; this rule was declared unconstitutional and abolished |
| Restructuring clause — §8d KStG | Losses preserved if business operations continue unchanged (Fortführungsgebundener Verlustvortrag) |

## 6. Provisions & Reserves

### Tax-Deductible Provisions (Rückstellungen) — §5 Abs. 1 EStG / §249 HGB

| Provision Type | Deductibility | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Provisions for uncertain liabilities | Deductible | Must be probable and estimable — §249 Abs. 1 Satz 1 HGB |
| Warranty provisions | Deductible | Based on historical experience |
| Pension provisions (Pensionsrückstellungen) | Deductible | §6a EStG — discounted at **6%** fixed rate |
| Environmental / decommissioning | Deductible | If legal or constructive obligation exists |
| Provisions for outstanding invoices | Deductible | |
| Tax provisions | Deductible | For estimated tax liabilities other than income tax |

### Restrictions

| Restriction | Detail |
|---|---|
| Discounting | Long-term provisions (>12 months) must be discounted at **5.5%** — §6 Abs. 1 Nr. 3a Buchst. e EStG |
| Provisions for future expenses | **Not deductible** — Aufwandsrückstellungen are not permitted for tax purposes |
| General reserves | **Not deductible** |

## 7. Industry-Specific Deductions

### Shipping (Tonnage Tax) — §5a EStG

| Parameter | Value |
|---|---|
| Method | Lump-sum profit determination based on net tonnage |
| Effective rate | Significantly below standard CIT rate |
| Commitment period | **10 years** minimum election |
| Eligible | Shipping companies operating vessels in international trade |

### Real Estate

| Parameter | Value |
|---|---|
| Special depreciation for rental housing | **5%** p.a. for 4 years for new buildings with construction cost ≤ €5,200/m² (§7b EStG) |
| Heritage building restoration | Up to **9%** p.a. for 8 years + 7% for 4 years on restoration costs (§7i EStG) |

### Film and Media

| Parameter | Value |
|---|---|
| DFFF (German Federal Film Fund) | Grants up to 20% of eligible German production costs |
| Tax treatment | Grants reduce depreciable base |

### Banking and Insurance

| Parameter | Value |
|---|---|
| Bank levy (Bankenabgabe) | Deductible as business expense |
| Insurance technical provisions | Deductible per special rules under §§20–21 KStG |

## 8. Treaties & Transfer Pricing

### Transfer Pricing — §1 AStG (Außensteuergesetz)

| Parameter | Detail |
|---|---|
| Standard | Arm's-length principle — §1 Abs. 1 AStG |
| Documentation | Mandatory contemporaneous documentation for cross-border related-party transactions — §90 Abs. 3 AO |
| Local file / Master file | Required for taxpayers with cross-border transactions >€6M (goods) or >€600,000 (services) per year |
| Penalty for non-compliance | Surcharges of 5–10% of income adjustment; minimum €5,000 — §162 Abs. 4 AO |
| Country-by-Country Reporting | Required for MNE groups with revenue ≥€750M — §138a AO |
| Advance Pricing Agreements | Available (bilateral preferred) — §89a AO |

### Treaty Network

Germany has an extensive network of over **95** double taxation agreements.

| Income Type | Typical Treaty Rate |
|---|---|
| Dividends (substantial holding) | 0–5% |
| Dividends (portfolio) | 15% |
| Interest | 0–10% |
| Royalties | 0–10% |

### CFC Rules — §§7–14 AStG

| Parameter | Value |
|---|---|
| Threshold | >50% German ownership of low-taxed foreign entity |
| Low-tax test | Effective tax rate < **25%** |
| Passive income | Attributed to German shareholders (Hinzurechnungsbesteuerung) |

## 9. Non-Deductible Items

| Expense | Statutory Reference |
|---|---|
| Corporate income tax (KSt) and solidarity surcharge | §10 Nr. 2 KStG |
| Gewerbesteuer | **Deductible** for corporate income tax but added back for GewSt base |
| Gifts to business contacts > **€50** per person p.a. | §4 Abs. 5 Satz 1 Nr. 1 EStG (increased from €35 to €50 from 2024) [unverified — confirm with local counsel] |
| Entertainment (30% of reasonable business entertainment non-deductible) | §4 Abs. 5 Satz 1 Nr. 2 EStG |
| Guest houses / hunting, fishing, yachts, sailing | **Fully non-deductible** — §4 Abs. 5 Satz 1 Nr. 3–4 EStG |
| Fines and penalties (criminal, administrative) | **Fully non-deductible** — §4 Abs. 5 Satz 1 Nr. 8 EStG |
| Bribes | **Fully non-deductible** — §4 Abs. 5 Satz 1 Nr. 10 EStG |
| Supervisory board remuneration | **50% non-deductible** — §10 Nr. 4 KStG |
| Dividends paid | Not an expense; distribution of profits |
| Hidden profit distributions (verdeckte Gewinnausschüttungen) | **Non-deductible** — §8 Abs. 3 Satz 2 KStG |
| Excessive vehicle costs (private use element) | 1% rule or logbook method for mixed-use — §6 Abs. 1 Nr. 4 EStG |
| Capital expenditure | Must be depreciated; no immediate deduction (except GWG/§7g) |
