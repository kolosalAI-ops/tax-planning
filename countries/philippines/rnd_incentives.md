# Philippines - R&D Tax Incentives

## 1. Overview

| Feature | Details |
|---|---|
| **Primary Incentive** | 100% Additional Deduction (200% total) for local R&D under CREATE Act |
| **Enhanced Benefits** | CREATE MORE Act (RA 12066) further enhancements |
| **PEZA Incentive** | Up to 7-year CIT holiday for registered enterprises |
| **BOI Benefits** | Income tax holiday and special CIT rate for registered activities |
| **Governing Authority** | Bureau of Internal Revenue (BIR); Board of Investments (BOI); Philippine Economic Zone Authority (PEZA); Fiscal Incentives Review Board (FIRB) |
| **Key Legislation** | CREATE Act (RA 11534) Art. 34; CREATE MORE Act (RA 12066); Tax Code (NIRC); Fiscal Incentives Code |
| **Corporate Tax Rate** | 25% (standard); 20% (domestic corps with net taxable income ≤ PHP 5M and total assets ≤ PHP 100M) |

## 2. Detailed Programs

### 2.1 Enhanced Deduction for R&D - CREATE Act (RA 11534) Art. 34

**Legal Basis:** Republic Act No. 11534 (CREATE Act), Section 34; Revenue Regulations implementing R&D deduction

| Parameter | Details |
|---|---|
| **Base Deduction** | 100% (standard expense deduction) |
| **Additional Deduction** | 100% of qualifying R&D expenditure |
| **Total Deduction** | 200% of qualifying local R&D expenditure |
| **Location** | R&D must be conducted locally in the Philippines |
| **Eligibility** | All domestic corporations and resident foreign corporations |
| **Effective Date** | April 11, 2021 (effectivity of CREATE Act) |

**Qualifying R&D Expenditure:**
- Salaries of R&D personnel
- Materials and supplies consumed in R&D
- Equipment depreciation allocated to R&D
- Payments to local research institutions
- Patent and IP costs from R&D activities

**Conditions:**
- R&D must be directly connected to the taxpayer's trade or business
- Must be conducted within the Philippines
- Must seek to discover new knowledge or develop new products/processes
- Routine quality control and market research do not qualify

### 2.2 CREATE MORE Act (RA 12066) Enhancements

**Legal Basis:** Republic Act No. 12066 (CREATE MORE Act), signed into law November 11, 2024

| Parameter | Details |
|---|---|
| **Enhanced R&D Deduction** | Up to 200% additional deduction (300% total) for priority activities |
| **FIRB Role** | Fiscal Incentives Review Board oversees enhanced incentive grants |
| **Scope Expansion** | Broader definition of qualifying R&D activities |
| **Registered Enterprises** | Additional incentives for BOI/PEZA registered R&D activities |
| **Effective Date** | 2025 (implementing rules pending) |

**Key CREATE MORE Enhancements:**
- Rationalized fiscal incentives framework
- Enhanced deductions for R&D conducted in collaboration with Philippine universities
- Extended incentive periods for strategic investments
- Streamlined application process through FIRB

### 2.3 PEZA Registered Enterprises

**Legal Basis:** Special Economic Zone Act of 1995 (RA 7916, as amended); CREATE Act revisions

| Parameter | Details |
|---|---|
| **Income Tax Holiday** | 4-7 years (depending on location and industry) |
| **Special CIT Rate** | 5% of gross income earned after ITH period |
| **Enhanced Deductions** | Additional 50-100% deduction for specific costs (training, infrastructure) |
| **R&D Activities** | PEZA-registered R&D enterprises eligible for ITH |
| **Zones** | Export processing zones, IT parks, manufacturing zones |

**PEZA Incentive Tiers Under CREATE:**

| Tier | ITH Period | Location | Activity Type |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Tier I** | 4 years | Metro Manila | Export/IT |
| **Tier II** | 5 years | Adjacent areas | Export/IT |
| **Tier III** | 6 years | Other areas with infrastructure | Export/IT |
| **Tier IV** | 7 years | Least developed areas | Export/IT |

### 2.4 BOI Registered Activities

**Legal Basis:** Omnibus Investments Code (EO 226); CREATE Act revisions

| Parameter | Details |
|---|---|
| **Income Tax Holiday** | 4-7 years for registered activities |
| **Special CIT Rate** | 5% of gross income post-ITH (export enterprises) |
| **Enhanced Deductions** | Additional deductions for qualifying costs |
| **Investments Priority Plan** | Activities listed in annual IPP eligible for incentives |
| **R&D as Priority** | R&D activities consistently included in IPP |

### 2.5 Philippine Innovation Act (RA 11293)

| Parameter | Details |
|---|---|
| **Purpose** | Framework for national innovation ecosystem |
| **R&D Target** | Government target of 2% GDP for R&D spending |
| **Innovation Fund** | National Innovation Council and innovation fund established |
| **Tax Linkage** | Complements CREATE Act R&D tax incentives |
| **Startup Support** | Innovative Startup Act (RA 11337) provides additional support |

## 3. Patent Box / IP Regime

The Philippines does not have a formal patent box regime.

| Aspect | Status |
|---|---|
| **Patent Box** | Not available |
| **IP Amortization** | Amortized over useful life under NIRC provisions |
| **IP Transfer** | Subject to CIT on gains; capital gains tax may apply |
| **Royalty WHT** | 20% final tax on domestic royalties; 25-30% on non-resident payments (reduced under treaties) |
| **PEZA Alternative** | PEZA-registered IP activities may benefit from 5% special CIT rate |
| **Alternative** | Enhanced R&D deduction and PEZA/BOI incentives support IP development |

**Note:** The Philippines has approximately 44 tax treaties that may reduce royalty WHT rates. Cross-border IP structures should consider treaty benefits alongside domestic incentives.

## 4. Effective Benefit Analysis (per $1M USD R&D Spend)

*Assuming PHP/USD exchange rate of 56; PHP 56M equivalent spend; 25% CIT rate*

| Scenario | Treatment | Tax Saving (PHP) | Tax Saving (USD) |
|---|---|---|---|
| **CREATE Enhanced (200% total)** | PHP 56M × 100% additional × 25% | PHP 14.0M additional | ~$250,000 |
| **Standard Deduction Only** | PHP 56M × 100% × 25% | PHP 14.0M | ~$250,000 |
| **Total with Enhanced** | PHP 56M × 200% × 25% | PHP 28.0M total | ~$500,000 total |
| **PEZA ITH (0% CIT)** | Full CIT exemption | Full CIT saving | Up to $250,000/year |
| **PEZA Post-ITH (5% GIE)** | 5% gross income | Significant reduction | Varies by gross income |
| **CREATE MORE (300% if enacted)** | PHP 56M × 200% additional × 25% | PHP 28.0M additional | ~$500,000 additional |

**Combined Example (PEZA + Enhanced Deduction):**
- During ITH: CIT exempted; enhanced deduction has no CIT benefit (but losses may carry forward)
- Post-ITH: 5% GIE basis; enhanced deduction may not apply to GIE basis
- Companies must carefully analyze interaction between PEZA/BOI incentives and R&D deductions

**Key Insight:** The 200% total deduction under CREATE provides meaningful benefit for non-incentivized companies. For PEZA/BOI registered companies, the tax holiday is typically more valuable than the enhanced deduction during the holiday period.

## 5. Claim Process & Documentation

### 5.1 Enhanced Deduction Filing Process

| Step | Action | Timeline |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Identify qualifying local R&D activities | During fiscal year |
| 2 | Maintain separate R&D expenditure records | Ongoing |
| 3 | Prepare supporting documentation | Year-end |
| 4 | Claim enhanced deduction on annual income tax return (BIR Form 1702) | April 15 (calendar year taxpayers) |
| 5 | Retain records for BIR audit | Minimum 10 years |

### 5.2 PEZA/BOI Registration Process

| Step | Action | Timeline |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Determine eligibility and select PEZA or BOI | Pre-registration |
| 2 | Submit registration application | Before commencing operations |
| 3 | PEZA/BOI evaluation | 20-60 days |
| 4 | Approval and issuance of registration certificate | Upon approval |
| 5 | FIRB endorsement (for enhanced incentives under CREATE) | Following PEZA/BOI approval |
| 6 | Annual compliance reporting | Annually |

### 5.3 Documentation Requirements

- **R&D project documentation:** Descriptions, objectives, methodology, outcomes
- **Expenditure records:** Itemized R&D costs with supporting invoices and receipts
- **Personnel records:** Employment contracts, time allocation for R&D staff
- **Local R&D evidence:** Proof that activities were conducted in the Philippines
- **BIR registration:** Updated BIR certificate of registration
- **PEZA/BOI certificate:** Registration and compliance certificates
- **Financial statements:** Audited by independent CPA
- **Transfer pricing documentation:** For related-party R&D arrangements (per RR 2-2013)

## 6. Recent Changes & Sunset Provisions

| Change | Effective Date | Details |
|---|---|---|
| **CREATE Act (RA 11534)** | April 11, 2021 | Introduced 200% R&D deduction; reduced CIT to 25% |
| **CREATE MORE Act (RA 12066)** | November 11, 2024 | Enhanced fiscal incentives; expanded R&D benefits |
| **FIRB Establishment** | 2021 | Centralized fiscal incentive oversight |
| **PEZA Incentive Rationalization** | 2021 | Tiered ITH system based on location |
| **Implementing Rules (CREATE MORE)** | 2025 (expected) | Detailed rules for enhanced R&D deductions |
| **Strategic Investment Priority Plan** | Annual updates | Updated list of priority investment activities |

**Sunset Provisions:**
- CREATE Act R&D deduction: **No explicit sunset** - permanent amendment to Tax Code
- PEZA/BOI incentives: Tiered with specific duration per registration
- CREATE MORE enhancements: Subject to implementing rules (IRR) issuance
- FIRB may review and adjust incentive terms periodically
- Transition rules for existing PEZA/BOI registrants under CREATE framework
